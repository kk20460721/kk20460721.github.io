When you understand what renunciation is, you understand this is neti, neti.
You say, "I am not this body, because I am aware of the body; the very awareness
makes me separate and different.' Go deeper. Go on peeling the onion: "I am not
the thoughts, because they come and go but I remain. I am not the emotions... "
They come, sometimes very strong, and you forget yourself completely in them,
but they go. There was a time they were not, you were; there was a time they
were, and you were hidden in them. There is again a time when they have gone
and you are sitting there. You cannot be them. You are separate.
Go on peeling the onion: no, body you are not; thinking you are not; feeling you
are not. And if you know that you are not these three layers, your ego simply
disappears without leaving a trace behind -- because your ego is nothing but
identification with these three layers. Then you are, but you cannot say "I." The
word loses meaning. The ego is not there; you have come home.
This is the meaning of sannyas: it is negating all that you are not but are
identified with. This is the surgery. This is the destruction.
