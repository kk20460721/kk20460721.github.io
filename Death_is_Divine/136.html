Gorakh says, keep your soul. Let your ego go, let your being remain. Being and ego are two different
things. Ego is your illusion. It is a process you have created. And being is the grace of god. Being
is what you have brought with you, ego is what you have acquired. Being is like a mirror, only a
witness. Ego has become a doer, it suffers and enjoys.
