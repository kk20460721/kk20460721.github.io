For these people, Prabhupad, the founder of Hare Krishna Movement... He is almost like a magnet:
he attracts the uttermost idiots in the world! That much credit must be given to him. Muktananda –
he is not that much great as Prabhupad but comes very close. Now he is sitting on the Miami Beach
– that is the right place, that’s where these people are needed.
