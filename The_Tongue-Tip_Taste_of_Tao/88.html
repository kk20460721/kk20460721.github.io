CHAPTER 15.
So be a river, ﬂowing towards the ocean, towards god, the inﬁnity, and yet be utterly in love with the
banks you will be passing by... the trees, the mountains, the days and the nights and the people. Be
utterly in love with all that and yet don’t get entangled anywhere; let the ﬂow remain unhindered.
One who can manage love and freedom together is wise, and that becomes possible through
meditation. Meditation is the key that unlocks love, unlocks freedom; it is a master key.
