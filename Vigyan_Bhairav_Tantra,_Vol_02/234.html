CHAPTER 17. SEARCHING FOR FREEDOM
This sutra says:
THINKING NO THING
WILL LIMITED-SELF UNLIMIT
You will become unlimited.
You will become whole.
You will become universal.
You will be
everywhere.
And then you are joy.
Now you are nothing but misery.
Those who are cunning,
they go on deceiving themselves that they are not miserable, or they go on hoping that something
will change, something will happen, and they will achieve at the end of life – but you are miserable.
You can create faces, deceptions, false faces; you can go on smiling continuously, but deep down
you know you are in misery. That is natural. Conﬁned in thoughts you will be in misery. Unconﬁned,
beyond thoughts – alert, conscious, aware, but unclouded by thoughts – you will be joy, you will be
bliss.
Vigyan Bhairav Tantra, Vol 2
234
Osho
