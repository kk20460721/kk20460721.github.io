you, an absence, and you feel something is missing. This is disease in the beginning. First it will be
felt in the mind. As I told you, ﬁrst it will be felt in the unconscious.
You may not be aware of it; in your dreams it will come ﬁrst; in your dreams you will see illness,

feeling that you are ill, and then it moves to the body. It has been always moving to the body, and

And now he says that he can predict that within six months there will be a tumor.
