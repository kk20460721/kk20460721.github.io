The ﬁfth question:
Question 5
I OFTEN GET THE FEELING THAT YOUR HANDS ARE SPEAKING TO US, AND THEY ARC
SAYING SOMETHING DIFFERENT FROM YOUR WORDS. ARE YOU POINTING AT THE MOON?
You have caught me right – and red-handed. Try to understand it. Not only by my hands, by every
gesture I am saying something, if you can decipher it and decode it. When I am talking, I am saying
something, when I am not saying anything, then too I am saying something. When my hands are
moving I am saying something, when they are not moving then too I am saying something.
You will need to be more and more alert so that you can listen to me as a totality.
The sixth question:
Question 6
WHEN YOU LOOK AT US WHAT EXACTLY DO YOU SEE?
This question is from Pratima. I see the ﬁrst layer, the Pratima which is not real – the pretension, the
effort to show something that is not there.
Then at the second layer I see another Pratima, which is there, but which the ﬁrst layer is trying to
hide, to suppress, to push into the dark.
Then I see the third layer which is so unconscious that the ﬁrst two layers are completely oblivious
of it.
