Even the Himalayas come and go
8 October 1988 pm in Gautam the Buddha Auditorium
BELOVED OSHO,
ONCE, NANSEN DECIDED TO VISIT A VILLAGE, BUT WAS VERY SURPRISED TO FIND THAT
PREPARATIONS HAD BEEN MADE TO WELCOME HIM.
NANSEN SAID TO THE VILLAGE HEAD, ”IT HAS BEEN MY CUSTOM NEVER TO LET ANYONE
KNOW BEFOREHAND ABOUT WHERE I AM JOURNEYING TO. HOW COULD YOU KNOW THAT
I WAS COMING TO VISIT YOUR VILLAGE TODAY?”
THE VILLAGE HEAD REPLIED, ”LAST NIGHT, IN A DREAM, THE GOD OF THE SOIL-SHRINE
REPORTED TO ME THAT YOU WOULD COME TO VISIT TODAY.”
NANSEN REPLIED, ”THIS SHOWS HOW WEAK AND SHALLOW MY SPIRITUAL LIFE IS THAT IT
CAN BE FORESEEN BY VISIONS!”
Maneesha, before discussing your sutras I have to discuss something about Nansen’s way of
tackling things, his methodology.

statements on purpose. Unless you understand that, it will be difﬁcult to ﬁnd any rationality in the

grasp something of the beyond.
