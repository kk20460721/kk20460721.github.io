A friend wanted to pick a ﬂower and give it to George Bernard Shaw. They were strolling together
in a garden. The friend’s garden, the ﬂowers... He saw Bernard Shaw standing near the ﬂowers
overwhelmed, absorbed... The friend started to pick. Bernard Shaw said, ”Stop! Stop! Don’t pick
them.”
He said, ”Don’t you like the ﬂowers?”
Bernard Shaw said, ”I like them very much, they are very lovely.”
So he said, ”That’s why I am picking them.”
