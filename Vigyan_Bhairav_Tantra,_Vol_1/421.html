Accepting the total life means the middle path.
If you deny, you move to the opposite extreme.
Denial is extreme. If you deny anything, you deny it for something; then you move to one extreme.
If one denies sex, he will move to BRAHMACHARYA, celibacy - to the other extreme. If you deny
brahmacharya, you will move to indulgence - to the other extreme. The moment you deny, you have
accepted the extreme path.
Acceptance of totality is to be automatically in the middle. You are neither for something nor against
something. You have not chosen, you are just ﬂoating in the stream. You are not moving toward a
goal – you have no choice. You are in a let-go.
Tantra believes in a deep let-go. When you choose, your ego comes in. When you choose, your
