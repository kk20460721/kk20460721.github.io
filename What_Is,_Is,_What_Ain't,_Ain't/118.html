If we all belong to one unity, how can one be superior, and how can one be inferior ? There is no
comparison possible because there is only one existence. With what to compare it? With whom to
compare it?
A really humble person knows no comparison. He will not say, ’I am more humble than you’ – that is
impossible for a humble person to say or even to think. He will say, ’I am you and you are me.’ And
when one can understand that one is so vast, misery cannot exist.
If all these trees and all these stars and all these rivers and oceans belong to you, how can you be
miserable? If this whole life – past, present, future – is your life, how can you be miserable? Then
where is death? There is no death... the whole is never going to die.
So a humble person becomes deathless. In love and humbleness, one overcomes death – death
disappears.
