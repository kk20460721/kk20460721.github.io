For example, if you can force somebody to do something, you will feel a sort of power. Love also

They simply do things because they love each other. You understand?
You can try to force the other to do certain things because you have seen lovers do those things. For
example two people who love each other, hug. Now you can manipulate a person to hug you, but
that doesn’t mean that there is love. In love hugging happens, but in hugging there is no necessity
for love to happen. You can manage the act but the inner feeling will be missing.
So just go in headlong like a fool. And sometimes angels miss what fools attain. And I am here...
just move...

you into a whirlwind of problems.
