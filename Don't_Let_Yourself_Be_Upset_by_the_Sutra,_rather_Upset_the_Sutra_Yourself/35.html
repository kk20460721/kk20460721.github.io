Enjoy it, nothing is wrong in it. Even sadness has a beauty of its own, it has a depth. In fact it is
far more important than your so called happiness, because that is very shallow. Your happiness is
shallow, your sadness is deep. It touches your very core. So don’t be worried; let it happen, watch
it.
