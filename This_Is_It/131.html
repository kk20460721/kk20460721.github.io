... Love and death are very deeply related, very deeply related – almost the same door; from one
side it is love, from another side it is death.
If your love life is going perfectly well, harmoniously, you will not think about death at all. People start
thinking about death only when something is going wrong in their love energy. When somebody is
celebrating his love then death is not a problem at all.
It arises only when you start feeling that love is drying up in some way, is getting dry and the juices
are no more ﬂowing. Then you are threatened – and the threat in that moment is of death, because
without love, a man is dead.
That’s why I wanted to know what is going on in your love affair.
