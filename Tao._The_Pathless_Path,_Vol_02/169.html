CHAPTER 9. THE MASTER IS A MUST
You have to go through it. Although you will be in search of somebody who can help, remember that
the help will not be a cure – nobody can cure it – the help will only be able to help you to understand
what is happening, to help you accept it, and fall in rapport with it so that the process becomes
speedy. Then the one who is almost a sage becomes a perfect sage.
