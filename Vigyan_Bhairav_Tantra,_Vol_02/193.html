Once a visitor asked many people in a small town about the mayor: ‘What type of man is your
mayor?’
Said the priest, ‘He is no good.’
Said the ﬁlling-station attendant, ‘He is a bum.’
And said the barber, ‘I never in my life voted for that rascal.’
Then the visitor met the mayor, a much maligned man, and the visitor asked, ‘What pay are you
getting for your job?’
Said the mayor, ‘My goodness, I am not getting any pay for it. I have accepted this job just for the
honor of it!’
