In giving you this name I am just giving you an indication, a ﬁnger pointing to the moon, to start
becoming more and more grateful... for small things. They are not small. Nothing is small... nothing
can be small because all is suffused with god. Feel grateful for everything – for a green leaf in the
wind, for a small bird singing alone, for a kite on the wing in the sky just relaxedly ﬂoating. All these
are great mysteries but because they are given to us free of any charge, we don’t take any note of
them.
If there were a price we would have hankered to see a kite, just relaxing high in the winds above the
clouds. If there were a price then people would have stood in a queue, would have waited long, and
would have felt very thankful.
Life is given without any charge: it is a gift. So start being more and more praise-full. That is the
meaning of ’chama’ – one who goes on singing in praise
