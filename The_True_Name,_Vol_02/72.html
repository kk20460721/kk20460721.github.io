AND DESTINY DETERMINES OUR JUST INHERITANCE.
Therefore be patient, be tranquil in whatever is given you, for it is a part of your destiny. Things had
to happen this way and so they happen. And since only that happens which has to happen, why

liberate yourself from unions and separations. This alone is the siddhi, all else is lesser quality stuff.
