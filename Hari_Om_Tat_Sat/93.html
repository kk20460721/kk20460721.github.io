because I have nothing to do with this business.

Jesus and Saint Peter come down to earth to see how things are going.
After traveling all day
through the universe they arrive after dark near an old farmhouse.
Not wanting to freak out the
farmer, they decide to sleep in the barn.
Jesus says to Peter, ”I am going to sleep upstairs in the hayloft and you stay down here. And when
you are comfortable, sing me a lullaby to help me go to sleep.” Peter agrees and starts singing softly.
”Sing louder!” calls out Jesus.
”But my Lord,” says Peter, ”the farmer may wake up.”
”Peter!” says Jesus, ”do you trust me?”
So Peter sings louder until the farmer wakes up, comes running into the barn and gives Peter a good
beating.
”Peter,” says Jesus, ”do you still trust me?”
