The third step is silence. I have called it ‘The Watcher on the Hills’. Become as silent as if you are
alone on the top of an Himalayan peak, utterly silent and alone, just watching, listening... sensitive,
but still.
And the fourth step is let-go.
At every step Nivedano will be asked to beat his drum. Yesterday he lost himself into the meditation.

the trees, Nivedano is ﬁghting for the rocks.
He did not listen, once he started...
