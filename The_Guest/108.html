Yes, something has happened that has never happened before: for the ﬁrst time in the evolution of
human consciousness man stands alienated from God. Man stands separated from existence. Man
stands lonely, with no companion, in great darkness, with no light to lead him, guide him. Man has
never been in such despair, man has never been in such a state of homelessness.
