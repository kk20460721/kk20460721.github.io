Socrates said, "Because you take yourself always with yourself. Wherever you go
you take yourself with yourself; that is the trouble. This time you go alone. Leave
yourself in Athens. Then there is a possibility of maturity."
If you drop the ego, there is a possibility you may become whole. The moment
you become whole, you become holy. Then you are healed; then all wounds
disappear. Then you are perfect in your total aloneness. It is not comparative --
that you are more perfect than others. No. You are simply perfect; you are a
unique piece; there is nobody else like you; you are only like yourself
In your wholeness you are perfect, and a deep contentment comes. It becomes a
climate around you.
