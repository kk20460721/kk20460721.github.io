of death that is looming on the horizon be avoided.
My people can do it! There is nobody else to whom this great responsibility can be given.
My people have no ambitions. They rejoice without any reason. They dance and sing – they don’t

Question 2
BELOVED OSHO,
I HEARD YOU SAY SOMETIME AGO THAT SCIENCE IS OF THE HEAD AND RELIGION IS
OF THE HEART. I UNDERSTAND THAT THESE QUALITIES, BEING OF A POLARITY, ARE
MUTUALLY DEPENDENT. ONE CANNOT EXIST WITHOUT THE OTHER, JUST AS MAN CANNOT
EXIST WITHOUT BOTH HEAD AND HEART. WOULD NOT THEN A WORLD SCIENTIFIC
COMMUNITY BRING WITH IT, AS A NECESSARY BY-PRODUCT, A WORLD RELIGIOUS
COMMUNITY? IS NOT THE VISION OF A WORLD SCIENCE AND A WORLD RELIGION
SYNTHESIZED IN YOUR VISION OF THE NEW MAN?
Man is not only head and heart. There is something more than both in him – his being.
So you have to understand three things: the head, the heart and the being.
