The final enlightenment can never be forced by anybody on you. It is your
freedom to choose it or reject it. At the most, I can give you an invitation; nothing
more can be done. And the whole problem is that you are not surrendering. On
the contrary, you are trying to use me; and these two things are totally different.
When you surrender, you surrender. Then you don't bother about the final
enlightenment. Then you say,'Whatsoever happens is good' If you want to use
me, then you are always worried:'The enlightenment is not happening, the
SAMADHI is not happening'; this is not happening, that is not happening. And
then, by and by, you start feeling a certain grudge, a complaint against me, as if I
am not pulling you out. Nobody can pull you out; only you can jump out of it. In
the first place, nobody has forced and placed you there. You have entered into it,
and you will have to come out of it. I can give you a hand, but you will have to
hold my hand. In fact, my hand will not bring you out; that will be just an
excuse.
