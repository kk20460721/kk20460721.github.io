OSHO, WHAT IS YOUR WORK?
KABIR, IT IS NOT WORK AT ALL. The very idea of work is irrelevant with me. It is not work because
there is no WILL behind it. I am not here to impose anything upon anybody. I am not here to convert
you to any ideology or to a certain way of life. I am not teaching a philosophy of life, I am not teaching
anything at all – I am not a teacher. I am simply sharing whatsoever has happened with me.
