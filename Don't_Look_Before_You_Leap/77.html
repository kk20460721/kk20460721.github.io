CHAPTER 14.
So my suggestion is: you decide. Risk – whatsoever decision you take, risk will be there – but from
this moment become mature, independent and decide. And remember always: if you go into hell
with your own decision you will be happy, even there, because it is your decision; you have chosen
it out of your freedom. And even if you are forced into heaven, you will not be happy there because
you have been forced into it and heaven will look like a prison.
Freedom is the ultimate value, and slowly slowly all of my sannyasins have to learn to live in freedom.
So let this be the beginning. One thing is clear: if you go there you are going with loving people;
there is no problem. If you want to stay here, the doors of the ashram are open for you; any moment
you can enter in and be part.
