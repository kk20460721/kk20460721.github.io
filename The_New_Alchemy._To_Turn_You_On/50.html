Both will be there if one is there.
The other cannot be separated; it can only be hidden.

being, the world of no-action: the world of existence, not of doing. All that we do is on the periphery
and al! that we are is at the center. We have to continuously move from the center to the periphery
to do some thing. Whenever you are doing something, you are on the periphery. Whatsoever you
are doing, U is on the periphery. When you are nondoing, not doing anything, then you are at the
center. Witnessing is a nonact. Meditation is a nonact.
