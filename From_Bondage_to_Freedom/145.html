I know about Indira Gandhi in India. She was very much interested in my ideas – we had a few
meetings. She told me, ”My power consists in having a few ﬁles.”
I said, ”I don’t understand. What do you mean?”
She said, ”I have ﬁles against each politician who can be a competitor to me, and he knows that I
will immediately expose him before the public, before the court. Rather than becoming the prime
minister, he will be behind bars.”
Those ﬁles now are in the hands of her son, Rajiv Gandhi. Rajiv Gandhi was never in politics, but
those ﬁles are enough: all old politicians are silent, knowing perfectly well that they can be caught
and exposed. They have committed every kind of crime.
You will be surprised...
