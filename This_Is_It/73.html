his disciples... of course in a very spiritual way.

Hitler’s logic is that of a murderer and gandhi’s logic is that of a suicidal person – but suicide is

If I tell you ’Become a sannyasin otherwise I will commit suicide’, and then you become a sannyasin,

their ways are violent ways.

approach is that if I have a certain understanding, I have to explain it to you – and then too with the

not condemn you; that’s your individual freedom!
If you have decided to remain miserable, who am I to make you happy? If that is your decision then
I will accept it – it is your life! Freedom should not be interfered with in any way, for any reason.
