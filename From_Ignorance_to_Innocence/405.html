But Dandami said, ”Do you think your kingdom is too much? In a desert you could give it for one

I am not going
anywhere. If Aristotle wants to see a sannyasin he will have to come here. The thirsty go to the well,

Alexander had to accept it: ”Yes, I would give the whole kingdom for one glass of water.”
When water is scarce then of course rich people will be able to have control over water.

There is no need for war; there is no need for poverty. We have enough money, enough resources,

If that seventy percent is
prevented from going towards bringing death to humanity, there is no need for anybody to become

My idea is to raise every poor person higher and higher and bring him to the level of the richest
person. There is no need for poverty.
