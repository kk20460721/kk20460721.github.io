CHAPTER 7. SILENCE IS A SONG WITHOUT WORDS
When you ﬁnd such beautiful stories, and when you can understand them yourself, don’t make a
question out of them. You should bring questions which you cannot ﬁnd answers for. You should
bring questions which are torturing you and there is no way out.
Whatever I can say on this story will be simply elaboration, but it will not give you any more insight.
The story is complete in itself.
