VISUDDHA, the ﬁfth CHAKRA, is in the throat. VISUDDHA, the throat chakra, is the last point from
which you can fall. Up to that point there is a possibility of falling back. With the sixth chakra – the

born at the third eye centre. If you die at SAHASRAR, you will not be born again. But if you are at
the VISUDDHA, you will slip back to the ﬁrst, MULADHAR. In the next life you will have to start from
MULADHAR again.
