Buddha says: Not because of fear should you be moral, but because of
understanding; and not because of greed should you be moral -- because your
ordinary religions are based on fear and greed, just the ordinary trick of reward
and punishment. Just as you do with your children your religions have been
doing with you: if you do this you will go to heaven, if you do this you will go to
hell -- fear and greed. They are playing on human fear and greed, and they say
that one should not be afraid, and one should not be greedy, and their whole
structure is based on the same fear and greed.
Buddha says: Have no fear and have no greed. Just look into things, and out of
your awareness a responsibility arises. You start behaving gracefully. You don't
do foolish things, that's all.
