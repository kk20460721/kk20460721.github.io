CHAPTER 8. SECRETLY, A JEWEL IN HIS ROBE
and as vast
and as deep
as the sky itself.
This I call ”to be a message.”
Except becoming a buddha,
there is no way
to convey what you are experiencing.
And remember:
once a buddha,
forever a buddha.
Nivedano ...
(Drumbeat)
Call all the buddhas back.
Slowly and silently ...
sit for a few moments ...
just reﬂecting ...
collecting ...
remembering what has happened to you ...
where you have been.
Remember the route so you can go,
anytime you want,
into the temple.
You are the temple,
and deep inside you is the buddha.
Dogen, the Zen Master: A Search and a Fulﬁllment
