CHAPTER 23. BEING: THE MOST BEAUTIFUL FLOWER IN EXISTENCE
The missionary said, ”This is not enough, this is not the right gratitude. The right gratitude will be to
fall as many times into wells as you can. And if you can, inform me in advance; that will be really
wonderful.”
We are living in an insane world, and you have to ﬁnd some sanity within yourself. You cannot ﬁnd
it in the world, you can ﬁnd it only within yourself.
That sanity will be your ladder towards higher states of consciousness, bliss and ecstasies.
