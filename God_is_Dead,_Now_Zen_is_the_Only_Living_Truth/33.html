God is an insult to man
7 February 1989 pm in Gautam the Buddha Auditorium
OUR BELOVED MASTER,
WHEN SEKITO RECEIVED THE PRECEPTS, HIS MASTER, SEIGEN, ASKED HIM, ”NOW YOU
HAVE RECEIVED THE PRECEPTS, YOU WANT TO LEARN THE VINAYA, DON’T YOU?”
SEKITO REPLIED, ”THERE’S NO NEED TO LEARN THE VINAYA.”
SEIGEN ASKED, ”THEN, YOU WANT TO READ THE BOOK OF SHEELA?”
SEKITO REPLIED, ”THERE’S NO NEED TO READ THE BOOK OF SHEELA.”
SEIGEN ASKED, ”CAN YOU DELIVER A LETTER TO NANGAKU OSHO?”
SEKITO SAID, ”CERTAINLY.”
SEIGEN SAID, ”GO NOW, AND COME BACK QUICKLY. IF YOU COME BACK EVEN A LITTLE
LATE, YOU WILL MISS ME. IF YOU MISS ME, YOU CANNOT GET THE BIG HATCHET UNDER
MY CHAIR.”
SOON SEKITO REACHED NANGAKU. BEFORE HANDING OVER THE LETTER, SEKITO MADE
A BOW AND ASKED, ”OSHO, WHEN ONE NEITHER FOLLOWS THE OLD SAINTS NOR
EXPRESSES ONE’S INNERMOST SOUL, WHAT WILL ONE DO?”
