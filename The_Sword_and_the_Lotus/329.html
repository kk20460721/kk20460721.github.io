CHAPTER 24. THE MASTER IS AT THE DOOR
Now you don’t have anything! You and all that you have belongs to me. So if people take away a
few things, let me worry about it, you go on trusting.
But trust the whole of humanity.
Whatever happens, don’t lose your trust, because trust is so
valuable. It is going to give you all that meditation can give, all that prayer can give, and without
any effort.
And if you can trust one human being, you know the knack of it and you can trust
everybody.
My sannyasins have to remember it: trust everybody, because you have nothing to lose. But don’t
lose your trust, because that is such a great quality of your being that it will open your being to
godliness.
