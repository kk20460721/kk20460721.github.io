The sixth question:
Question 6
ARE ALL DESIRES THE SAME? WHAT IS MY DESIRE FOR LOVE?
IN THE ULTIMATE SENSE, all desires are the same -- because desire means you
are not contented with yourself as you are. Desire is a discontent.
Essentially, desire is a longing for that which is not. Essentially, desire is a
complaint against existence. You say: "This is not the way I want to be. This is not
the house I want to live in, and this is not the woman I want to love and be loved
by. This is not the world, this is not the society, this is not the body, this is not the
mind, that I can be content with."
Desire means discontent, and desire means a hope in the.future -- that
somewhere there must be a place where everything will fall in tune with you.
