Belief and auto-suggestion are the same thing.
Auto-suggestion is the scientiﬁc name for belief.
There is no difference between them.
However inner nature and belief are very different.
Inner nature is that which becomes manifest
when all beliefs have been dropped. When all thoughts and all beliefs have disappeared from your
mind, you experience your inner nature. Inner nature is not to be made a belief.
