And the moment you are identified with the watcher -- which is your real identity -- the
mind loses all its grip on you. Mind no more stirring, you have arrived to the truth.
This small sutra is very significant. Perhaps it may be that Ta Hui spoke it when he
realized -- not through his intellectual acumen but through his meditation -- the existential
truth. This may be the turning point from the intellectual into an enlightened being.
