Last night it rained cats and dogs. I have always wondered where this phrase may have
come from. There is only one possible source, and that is a fable of Aesop: A cat was very
happily walking by, smiling to herself, and a dog asked her, "What is the matter?"
She said, "Last night in my dreams it rained, and it rained rats!"
The dog said, "Listen, you stupid cat. You don't understand a thing about dream
psychology. I was also here last night and I can testify that it rained -- but it rained cats, not
rats."
