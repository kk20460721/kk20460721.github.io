I was reading about Baal Shem, a Hassid mystic, the founder of Hassidism. A
disciple came to him one day and said, "Master, how can I avoid temptation?
How can I avoid the Devil tempting me?"
Baal Shem looked at him and said, "Wait! There is no need for you to avoid any
temptation, because right now temptation cannot be given to you -- you are not
worthy of it."
He said, "What do you mean?"
He said, "Temptation comes only at the last moment. Right now the Devil is not
worried about you. In fact, the Devil is not chasing you at all -- you are chasing
the Devil -- so you don't be worried about temptation. It is not going to happen

do."
