So just reading and listening to me will not help much... start feeling. Listening, feel too not only
listen. Listening, listen too through the heart. Let it sink into your feeling aspect. That is the meaning
when all the religions say SHRADDHA – faith, trust – is needed. Trust means a way of listening from
the heart – not through doubt, not through logic, not through reasoning, not through discursive
intellect, but through a deep participation through the heart.
As you listen to music, listen to me that way. Don’t listen to me as you listen to a philosopher; listen
to me as you listen to the birds. Listen to me as you listen to a waterfall. Listen to me as you listen
to the wind blowing through the pines. Listen to me, not through the discursive mind, but through
the participant heart. And then something that you are continuously feeling is missing will not be
missed.
