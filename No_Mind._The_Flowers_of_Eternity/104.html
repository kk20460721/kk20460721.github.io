CHAPTER 10. WHEN I CALL YOU MY FRIENDS, I MEAN IT
Nivedano...
(Drumbeat)
Come back, remembering you are a buddha. Remembering the grace, the beauty, the silence. Sit
for a few moments just to recollect the golden path that you have traveled just now.
And in your day-to-day life remember as much as possible – without creating any tension and anxiety,
in a relaxed and restful way – that your every act becomes the act of one who is awakened, of one
who has tasted his innermost being.
I want thousands of buddhas around the world, and not a single Buddhist.
I teach you the buddha, but not Buddhism. I hate all ‘isms’, all religions. My love is for your eternity,
your immortal being.
I have called that immortal being within you, ”Mehre mehbub” – My Love, My Friend, My Beloved
One.
Okay, Maneesha?
Yes, Zorba The Buddha.
