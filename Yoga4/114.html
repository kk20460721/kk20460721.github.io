oriented.
Many more people attain to nirvana through Patanjali. Through Mahavir, rarely,
very few; the whole standpoint seems to be impossible. So listen to Patanjali well.
Not only listen, but try to imbibe the spirit. Much is possible through him. He's
one of the greatest scientists of the inner journey in the world.
