WHAT DO YOU SAY ABOUT THE THIRD WORLD WAR? IS IT GOING TO HAPPEN OR NOT?

Don’t do anything to prevent it, because this humanity that you see around the earth is not worth
anything.
A good third world war will at least clean this planet of human garbage:
cathedrals,
temples, mosques, White Houses where only lies are manufactured. Perhaps that’s why it is called

All these politicians, with no awareness, no love, and with all the nuclear weapons in their hands
....
The world right now contains so many nuclear and atomic weapons that this poor humanity can be

You can become the beginning of a new mankind. And the very newness of your consciousness will

possibility there will be no third world war. My sannyasins are not politicians. My sannyasins have
