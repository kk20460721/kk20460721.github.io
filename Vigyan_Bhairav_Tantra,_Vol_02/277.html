CHAPTER 20. LIBERATION FROM YOURSELF
Don’t say anything, don’t think about it. Don’t even say that this is sound. Just listen to it. Don’t
create any word around it. Don’t give it any name or form. Let is be as it is. Allow it to ﬂow, and you
be a witness. The stream ﬂows, and you are sitting on the bank, just a witness – not even knowing
the name of the stream, not knowing where it is going, not knowing from where it is coming.
Just sit near by the sound, and sooner or later it will disappear, and when it disappears there will be
silence. This is a good sign – you have touched a second layer – but if you try to think about it, you
will lose it; you will be thrown back to the ﬁrst. If you don’t think about it and can enjoy in witnessing,
you will go deeper to the third layer.
Vigyan Bhairav Tantra, Vol 2
