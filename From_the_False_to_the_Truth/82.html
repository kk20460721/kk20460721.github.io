I remember, a missionary had gone to the cannibals in Africa. He was the ﬁrst Christian missionary
who had dared to go to the cannibals to convert them to Christianity.
Cannibals are the most difﬁcult people to convert. They have not even dropped eating man, they
have eaten their own people. In the beginning – the beginning of this century – there were thousands
of cannibals in Africa. There are only three hundred left, because if you cannot get your food from
outside, you eat your children, you eat your old men – what else to do?
Their number goes on
decreasing; they will disappear. Perhaps the last cannibal will have to eat himself.

the cross, a miracle would happen

It was such a disappointment to
thousands of people who had come to see the miracle. It was a greater disappointment to Jesus
himself.

nothing stirred. Slowly people started going away, thinking that it was just a waste of time, the whole
day wasted in the heat.
