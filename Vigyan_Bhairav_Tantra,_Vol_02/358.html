CHAPTER 26. ENLIGHTENMENT – THE MOST ORDINARY
Hypnosis is all around. Education uses it, politics uses it, the market uses it – everyone is using
it. And if you are not aware then you are a victim. Become aware. If you become aware, you can
use it; not to hypnotize others, but to de-hypnotize yourself.
And if you can become completely
de-hypnotized, you are free, you are liberated.
And there is no conﬂict between meditation and hypnosis.
The conﬂict is in the directions – the
process is the same.
