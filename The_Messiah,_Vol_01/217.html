He said, ”Don’t you suggest to that man to stop?”
I said, ”He’s already so poor, in such suffering. Opium is the only thing that gives him dreams, and

And this is the situation of the whole of humanity. You go on and on hoping and hoping because you

If you drop your hopes, there will be a great revolution:
The birth of a new humanity.
