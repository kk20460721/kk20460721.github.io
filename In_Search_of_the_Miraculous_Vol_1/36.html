FOURTH STAGE: 10 MINUTES’ TOTAL REST
Now no questions and no deep breathing. Drop everything, abandon every effort. For these ten
minutes keep lying as if you are dead, as if you are not. Give up everything. For these ten minutes
drop all efforts and lie in waiting for him. Cease to do anything; neither ask ”Who am I?” nor breathe
deeply. Just keep lying – relaxed, restful. Listen to the roar of the sea. Listen to the wind passing
through the pines. If a bird calls, listen to its sound. For ten minutes feel as if you are dead, as if you
don’t exist.
And now open your eyes slowly, slowly. If your eyes don’t open, then cover them with your palms.
