One Polack arrived in New York seeking his fame and fortune. As he strolled down the
sidewalk he noticed a great long ladder propped against the side of a building,
stretching upward as far as the eye could see. He started to pass on by, but a voice high
in the clouds called down, "Climb up the ladder to success!"
Somewhat nervously he began to ascend, rung by rung, all the way to the top of the
fifty-story edifice. When he got there, a slender, blond, blue-eyed boy seated on the
ledge of the building smiled sweetly at him.
"Hi, there!" he said. "I am Cess!"
