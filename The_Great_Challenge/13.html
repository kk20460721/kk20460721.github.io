CHAPTER 1. FLIGHT OF THE ALONE TO THE ALONE
In sleep the mind goes to sleep. In samadhi the mind dies. So sleep and samadhi appear to be
similar but they are not, because after samadhi there is no survival of the mind. When you awaken
from your sleep in the morning your mind is strengthened, more fresh, and again duality is seen.
But after samadhi there is no coming back. This is the point of no return: you cannot come back.
Now the oneness will be eternal.
So samadhi and sushupti, dreamless sleep, have a similarity. You can say that samadhi is awakened
sushupti, or you can say that sushupti is a sleeping samadhi.
