CHAPTER 13. GOD IS THE OCEAN YOU ARE IN
I want you to destroy that duality completely. God is not the creator, but the creation itself. He is in
the trees and he is in the rivers and he is in the moon and he is in the sun and he is in you.
Except God, there is nothing else.
The seeker is the sought, the hunter is the hunted, the observer is the observed. And the moment
you realize this, such a deep relaxation comes, such a peace descends deep in you that you have
never dreamt of before. Such clarity comes to your eyes that everywhere you start seeing immense
beauty, immeasurable beauty, tremendous goodness. In the smallest things of life you start feeling
the heartbeat of the universe.
This is our temple, and this is our God, and we are part of it. The worshiper is not separate from the
worshiped.
To understand this organic unity is the true religion.
Okay, Vimal?
Yes, Osho.
