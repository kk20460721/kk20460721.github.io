]
Mm mm, it can be.
Start doing one thing every night: sit in a chair and rest your head (Osho
demonstrates, his head resting back on the chair like you do at the dentist). You can have a pillow,
mm? so you are in a resting posture. Then release your lower jaw, just relax it so the mouth opens
slightly, and start breathing from the mouth, not from the nose.
But the breathing has not to be
changed, it has to be as it is – natural.
The ﬁrst few breaths will become a little hectic. By and by it will settle down and the breathing will
become very shallow. It will go in and out very slightly; that’s how it should be, mm? Keep the mouth
open, eyes closed, and rest.
Then start feeling that your legs are becoming loose, as if they are being taken away from you,
broken from the joints. Feel as if they are being taken away from you, they have been cut loose,
broken, and then start thinking that you are just the upper part; the legs are gone.
Then the hands: think that both the hands are becoming loose and being taken away from you. You
may even be able to hear just a click! inside when they are broken. You are no more your hands –
they are dead, taken away. Then just the torso remains.
Then start thinking about the head – that it is being taken away, that you are being beheaded, that
the head is broken. Then leave it loose: wherever it turns – right, left – you cannot do anything. Just
leave it loose; it has been taken away. Then you have just your torso, mm? Feel that you are only
this much – this chest, the belly, that’s all.
