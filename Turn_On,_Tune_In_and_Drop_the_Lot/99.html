Going deep into sex always creates fear, so there are a few alternatives. One is that of the monk.

The other alternative is never to go deep in sex; so you make sex a superﬁcial thing. The polar

commitment, no involvement, so there is no fear.
But both are ugly. The monk is ugly and the prostitute is ugly. And both are together, they are not

Between the two, exactly in the middle, is the real life.
