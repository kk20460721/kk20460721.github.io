CHAPTER 1. THE TALE OF THE SANDS
THE STREAM WAS LEARNING. BUT THE SANDS WHISPERED, ”WE KNOW, BECAUSE WE
SEE IT HAPPEN DAY AFTER DAY: AND BECAUSE WE, THE SANDS, EXTEND FROM THE
RIVERSIDE ALL THE WAY TO THE MOUNTAIN.”
AND THAT IS WHY IT IS SAID THAT THE WAY IN WHICH THE STREAM OF LIFE IS TO
CONTINUE ON ITS JOURNEY IS WRITTEN IN THE SANDS.
Listen to the wisdom of the sands.
This story is of immense value. If you allow it to fall into your heart as a seed, soon it will grow into
a big tree. And when the time comes you will have great ﬂowers and great fragrance.
This is the story of sannyas. This is what I am doing here. This is what is happening here.
