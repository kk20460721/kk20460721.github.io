Dismantle the ego
17 October 1985 am in Rajneeshmandir
Question 1
BELOVED MASTER,
IS EMPATHY THE CURRENT THAT BRINGS ABOUT THE AWARENESS OF OUR EACH AND
EVERY RELATEDNESS, AND VICE VERSA?
The experience of empathy is very rare.
You know what sympathy means, you also know what
apathy means; but empathy you do not know.
Sympathy and apathy are opposed to each other. Empathy is beyond both. To help you understand
it, let me tell you an incident in Ramakrishna’s life.
He was in a boat, going to the other shore of the Ganges near Calcutta. And just out of nowhere –
he was surrounded by his disciples – he started screaming, ”Don’t beat me!” Tears started ﬂowing
from his eyes, and one could see that his body was getting distorted as if somebody was beating
him badly. The disciples could see nobody there, but he was in immense torture.
When they reached to the other shore, they found a ﬁsherman with a big crowd surrounding him. He
had been beaten by a few people. And the strangest thing was that the same bruises and scratches
that had come up on his back, had come up on Ramakrishna’s back too – exactly the same.
It was a very mysterious phenomenon.
They asked Ramakrishna what it was.
He said, ”This is
