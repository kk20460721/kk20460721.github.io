CHAPTER 22. TRUTH IS HERE BUT YOU ARE NOT
I want my truth to trigger something in you which is yours.
My truth simply gives you an assurance that a human being, just like you, can attain to truth.
That will give you immense respect towards yourself.
You will not feel unworthy sinners, that
somebody has to come to save you – some Christ, some Buddha has to come, and then you will be
saved. You don’t have to wait for anybody to save you. You are born saved, just a little insight
....
If
my presence can do that, then my work is ﬁnished.
I don’t want to become a prison around you.
I want to become a freedom around you, an open sky around you, not a golden cage – so that you
can open your wings and feel the joy of ﬂying higher and higher towards the stars.
