And what ideologies are you talking about?
What has religion to do with ideology?
The word
ideology consists of two words: idea and logic. Now, religion has nothing to do with ideas or with
logic. Ideas are mental, psychological.
Religion begins where ideas end.
Religion starts where ideas and their weight drop.
Religion has wings, but only if ideas are no longer top-heavy on it.
No ideas
....
and there is religion.
Your head is full of ideas, and there is no possibility of religion at all. Ideas simply mean you don’t
know and you are trying to ﬁgure out what it is. A blind man thinking about light; that is an idea. A
man who knows light has no idea about light, he simply knows light.
A religious man knows truth, reality, existence, as it is. He has no idea at all.
