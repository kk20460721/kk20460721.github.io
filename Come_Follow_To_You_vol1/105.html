RELIGION IS BASICALLY, essentially, a rebellion. It is not conformity, it is not
belonging to any organization, society, church, because all belonging comes out
of fear -- and religion is freedom. There is fear in being alone. One likes to belong
-- to a nation, tb a church, to a society, because when you belong to the crowd
you forget your loneliness. It does not disappear, but you become oblivious to it.
You deceive yourself, you create a dream around you, as if you are not alone.
You remain alone all the same; this is just an intoxicant.
