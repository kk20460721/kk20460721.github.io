CHAPTER 6. JOY IS INTUNEMENT
This is what I call trust, mm? Life brings loneliness – trust it. Life one day brings absolute aloneness
– trust it.
And as the trust grows and you settle with the new space, confusion will disappear.
Confusion is because you want to cling, you want to have something to cling to, and everything is
disappearing, the earth beneath you is disappearing and you are falling into an abyss. I want you to
fall into this abyss; this is what meditation is all about.
Just fall blissfully... Like a feather falling into an abyss. There is no bottom to it, but then one starts
enjoying it; just the -very fall is such a thrill.
Just wait – something good is happening!
Hallelujah!
