CHAPTER 12. DIE TO THE FUTURE
These feelings will certainly drop. ’Mine’, ’I’ – these have dropped long ago. It is at the dropping of
the ego that enlightenment has happened. To such a person there is no ’I’ or ’mine’ associated with
anything. Such a consciousness cannot even claim that ’I am soul’. He does not attach any sense
of ’I’ to anything; just the ’am-ness’ remains. We use the expression ’I am’; such a person uses the
expression ’am’. His ’I’ drops, only the state of pure ’am-ness’ remains.
This ’am-ness’ has been called the state of jivanamukta. When ’I’ dies and the ’am-ness’ remains,
you have attained to self-realization while living.
If our effort in this direction is free of desire this happening can take place right now. If desire is still
present, it takes time.
Enough for today.
