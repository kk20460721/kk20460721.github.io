CHAPTER 5. ON THE QUALITIES OF THE TAOIST
If you live too much in the dark you become allergic to light; if you live too much in impurity you
become allergic to purity. Says Lao Tzu: PURE WORTH APPEARS LIKE CONTAMINATED.
So don’t be afraid if you appear mad to other people; don’t be afraid if when you are meditating you
see others laughing – if they don’t laugh it wouldn’t be meditation at all.
Don’t be afraid of intellectuals who say: Listen to everybody but do always your own thing. Don’t be
bothered by them. If you hear the truth, if you feel the truth, then take the risk and go on the greatest
adventure – the adventure of truth, the adventure to seek God, the adventure to know oneself.
