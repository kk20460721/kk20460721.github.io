Suppose My Hand Were Always Like That?
14 December 1974 am in Buddha Hall
MOKUSEN HIKI WAS LIVING IN A TEMPLE IN THE PROVINCE OF TAMBA. ONE OF HIS
ADHERENTS COMPLAINED OF THE STINGINESS OF HIS WIFE.
MOKUSEN VISITED THE ADHERENT’S WIFE AND HELD HIS CLENCHED FIST BEFORE HER
FACE.
’WHAT DO YOU MEAN BY THAT?’ ASKED THE SURPRISED WOMAN. ’SUPPOSE MY HAND
WERE ALWAYS LIKE THAT, WHAT WOULD YOU CALL IT?’ ’DEFORMED,’ REPLIED THE
WOMAN.
THEN HE OPENED HIS HAND FLAT IN HER FACE AND ASKED: SUPPOSE IT WERE ALWAYS
LIKE THAT – WHAT THEN? ’ANOTHER KIND OF DEFORMITY,’ SAID THE WIFE.
’IF YOU UNDERSTAND THAT MUCH,’ FINISHED MOKUSEN, ’YOU ARE A GOOD WIFE.’ THEN
HE LEFT.
AFTER HIS VISIT THIS WIFE HELPED HER HUSBAND TO DISTRIBUTE AS WELL AS TO SAVE.
