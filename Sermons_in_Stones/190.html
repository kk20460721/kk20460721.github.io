CHAPTER 13. JEALOUSY – THE BYPRODUCT OF MARRIAGE
We are not searching for any paradise in the clouds. If it is there, we will get hold of it, but ﬁrst we
have to make a paradise here on the earth; that will be our preparation. If we can live in a paradise
on the earth, then wherever paradise is, it is ours; nobody else can claim it – at least not these
priests and monks and nuns! All these people are bound towards hell, because on the surface they
are one thing and inside it is just the opposite.
Try to be natural.
Risk everything to be natural and you will not be at a loss.
