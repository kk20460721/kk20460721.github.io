night after the Encounter group. It is a suﬁ meditation... the Suﬁs call it ’jikr’; it means remembrance.

This meditation will be useful.
Every night for twenty minutes sit on your bed and have the light off or very dim, and the fragrance
of some incense burning. You can go and ﬁnd the incense that Suﬁs burn in the mosque; that will
be very helpful. You have been a Mohammedan in your past life – that’s why I am giving it to you.
Sit silently, comfortably, and then for two or three minutes say, ’Allah

Allah’ loudly, with the lips
used. In the second step just the tongue is used inside and the lips are kept closed. In the third step

with it.
The ﬁfth step has nothing to do with you.
