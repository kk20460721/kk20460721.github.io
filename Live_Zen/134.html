Beat the drum
5 May 1988 pm in Gautam the Buddha Auditorium
BELOVED OSHO,
KASAN SAID, ”LEARNING BY STUDY IS CALLED HEARING; LEARNING NO MORE IS CALLED
NEARNESS; TRANSCENDING THESE TWO IS TRUE PASSING.”
A MONK ASKED, ”WHAT IS TRUE PASSING?”
KASAN SAID, ”BEATING THE DRUM.”
THE MONK ASKED AGAIN, ”WHAT IS THE TRUE TEACHING OF THE BUDDHA?”
KASAN SAID, ”BEATING THE DRUM.”
THE MONK ASKED ONCE MORE, ”I WOULD NOT ASK YOU ABOUT THIS VERY MIND IS THE
BUDDHA, BUT WHAT IS NO MIND, NO BUDDHA?”
KASAN SAID, ”BEATING THE DRUM.”
THE MONK STILL CONTINUED TO ASK: ”WHEN AN ENLIGHTENED ONE COMES, HOW DO
YOU TREAT HIM?”
KASAN SAID, ”BEATING THE DRUM.”
