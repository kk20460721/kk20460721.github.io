Question 13
DEAR OSHO, HELLO.
I will tell you an anecdote.
A persistent man said that he would teach a parrot to say 'hello' in an hour. He
went close to the parrot's cage, and started saying,'Hello, hello.' This went on for
a full thirty minutes without the parrot giving the slightest attention. At last the
bird turned his head slowly, blinked at the man, and said,'Number engaged.'
I will not say anything else about it.
You say 'hello'? You don't know; it can be said to me only through your being.
Otherwise, the number is engaged. Words won't reach me. You will have to say
them through your total being; otherwise, the number is engaged.
