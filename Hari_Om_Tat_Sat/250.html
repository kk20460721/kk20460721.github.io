Sagaresh, the vipassana meditation was invented by Gautam Buddha, and for twenty-ﬁve centuries
Buddhists have been torturing themselves. Now, who told you to go to Dharmagiri to S.N. Goenka,
to learn a meditation for which the whole context is missing? The meditation was perfectly right for
a man like Gautam Buddha. Always remember, everything is related, interdependent with a certain
context.
