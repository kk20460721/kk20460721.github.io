Holston was hired as a ranch hand in Texas. One day he approached Davis, the
foreman. "What do you do for fun here on the prairie?"
"Well," replied the foreman, "we got a Mexican cook on the ranch and every Saturday
night we dress him in women's clothes and six of us take him dancing."
"Not me!" declared Holston. "I don't go for that kind of stuff."
"Neither does the Mex," says Davis, "that's why it takes six of us."

Discipline is totally different. Discipline is out of your own choice; it is out of your own
will. Discipline, the very word, comes from a root which means learning. Discipline
