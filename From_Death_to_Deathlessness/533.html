CHAPTER 38. ENJOY THIS ORGASMIC MOMENT
As far as the plastic lady is concerned, we will make it as natural as you want. It is a very simple
phenomenon: if you want her to nag you, she will nag you; she will throw clothes at you. She just
has to carry a recording, a tape within herself, and she will call you all the dirty words that you are
accustomed to. And while you are making love to her, she will shriek and say, ”My Jesus!”
