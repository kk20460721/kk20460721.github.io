CHAPTER 5. A STILL MIND: THE DOOR TO THE DIVINE
Stand still! I don’t mean your body: let your mind stand still, your consciousness, and suddenly you
will become aware of something which has always been there. You have been seeking for it, seeking
and searching, lives and lives running for it – and it was here. It is so near, and that’s why you have
missed it. It is just by the corner, and you have sought it everywhere except this place where you
are standing.
Non-wavering reveals to you the here and now. That standing still in consciousness reveals to you
the presence which is here.
