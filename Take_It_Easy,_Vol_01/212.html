CHAPTER 11. THE LOTUS FLOWER OF THE LAW
Create! Let creativity be your religion, and you will never be lost again. The more you create, the
more you will grow in. It happens both the ways. If you create more, you grow in more. If you grow
in more, you create more.
If religion is just negative and life-denying it becomes ugly, it becomes a monster. When religion is
creative and helps you to see the greenness of the world and the rosing of the roses and helps you
to hear these birds in a crystal-clear way, helps you to feel and be sensitive to the silences, pauses
in existence, to the sounds... helps you to be in such a harmony that you can feel the harmony of
the whole
....
To feel the harmony of the whole is to become holy.
