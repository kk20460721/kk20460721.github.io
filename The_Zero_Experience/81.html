same. It has not been a cleansing process, it has not thrilled you from corner to corner, from end
to end. It has not vibrated you, because in that vibration you lose much dust, and in that vibration
there comes a moment where thoughts disappear and sex becomes almost ecstatic.
When thoughts are not there it is meditation, and that is the whole beauty of sex, that is the very
centre of sex. Otherwise there is a periphery and the centre is missing; then you are just making
empty gestures, empty motions.
People are making love and their minds are full of thoughts; it is not possible to attain to a deep bliss
through it. When thoughts completely disappear, for a moment everything stops: time stops, mind
stops. For a moment you don’t know who you are, for a moment you don’t know what your boundary
is, for a moment you don’t know where you are. You are in a nowhere! For a moment you have fallen
into eternity. The past has suddenly been cut off, there is no future and this moment is all!
Ordinarily our situation is that the past, the whole load, the whole train of the past, is following and
the whole projection of the future is there. The present moment is so tiny, so small.
Sex is one of the natural ways to enter into the now, the nowness of things.

certainly you are missing something very essential, something very signiﬁcant and it is not possible
