CHAPTER 13. WHEN THE COIN DISAPPEARS
Mulla Nasruddin became very excited, his blood pressure rose high. Even in the dark you could have
seen his eyes, they became so ﬁery. And then when Sheikh Abdullah said, ”Now the measurements
of my body – thirty-six, twenty-four, thirty-six.” Nasruddin jumped over to Sheikh Abdullah. Sheikh
Abdullah said, ”Wait, guess!”
Nasruddin said, ”Who is bothered about guessing? I don’t care who you are. Quick! Kiss me!”
The human mind is such – imagination, desire, passion, projection. You project, you imagine, and
then you become the victim. And this is not a joke, this is reality – and this is about you.
Enough for today.
