CHAPTER 6. GOD IS THE ORIGINAL SINNER

Be silent...
Close your eyes, and feel your body to be completely frozen.
This is the right moment to go inwards. Gather all your energies, gather your total consciousness
and rush towards your center of being with an urgency, as if this is the last moment of your life.
Only with such urgency have people become enlightened.
Faster and faster...
Deeper and deeper...
As you come closer to your center, a great silence descends over you. It is falling almost like soft
rain, cool, very tangible, and very mysterious.
Gautama the Buddha Auditorium has become absolutely silent, as if there is no one here at all.
A little deeper, closer to your being. And suddenly you are surprised, great fountains of peace burst
all around you.
A little closer, and there arises for the ﬁrst time a divine drunkenness, a deep ecstasy, a blissfulness
you have never known before.
One step more, and you are at the very center. Suddenly you see you are no more.
At the center is your hidden nature, your original face.
We have used Gautam Buddha’s face as a symbol for everybody’s original face. So let me say, you
have disappeared, only buddha is. In other words, you are no more, only existence is. And this is
the greatest experience, the highest peak of consciousness and the greatest depth, simultaneously.
The only thing you have to remember at this point is buddha consists of only one element,
consciousness, awareness, witnessing – different names of witnessing.
Just witness the way a mirror mirrors – no judgment, no appreciation, no identiﬁcation.
Witness you are not the body.
God is Dead, Now Zen is the Only Living Truth 165
