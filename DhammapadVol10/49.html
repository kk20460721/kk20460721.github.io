Desire is our imprisonment. The man who wants nothing, who is absolutely contented
as he is, is free of all bondage. He has attained to ultimate freedom, nirvana -- and that
is the goal of life. And it is only by attaining that freedom that you will know the
significance of being, the song of being, the celebration of being. Your life will become a
continuous bliss, and not only that YOU will be blissful, you will be able to bless others
too. The whole existence will be blessed by you, by your very presence.
Enough for today.
