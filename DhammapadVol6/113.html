Man moves almost in the same circles. Man as he is, is not sane, cannot be called sane.
But because insanity is so widespread, it is so normal that we don't become aware of
it
....
Once YOU become awakened then you are surprised how people are living, what
they are doing to themselves and to others. Their whole life is nothing but sheer
madness. Somebody is mad after money, somebody is mad after power, somebody is
mad after fame -- and all these things are futile.
Death comes, and the whole edifice that you have built with such labor collapses. Death
comes and takes you away, and all that you have created has been in vain.
The sane person is one who creates something which even death cannot destroy. Let
this be the definition of the sane: one who knows something of immortality,
deathlessness, eternity -- he is sane, he is a buddha. To be a buddha simply means to be
sane. One who is not aware of immortality and lives in time and thinks only in terms of
this world is insane. He is not aware of himself, how can he be sane?
We have not yet been able to create a sane society for the simple reason that we have
not been able to create many buddhas. The more buddhas there are in the world, the
more is the possibility of humanity rising to higher altitudes of being, understanding,
love, compassion. Otherwise you go on moving from one nightmare into another.
