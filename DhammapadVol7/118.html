The whole humanity is boiling within. People are so afraid, they are just somehow
managing to keep their faces together. Charlie's statement: "Give me two pickets to..." is
not really complete. The complete sentence will be: "Give me two pickets to Tittsburgh."
That is left out because the joke has been compiled by a man who must have been afraid
himself. It is not a complete joke. "Tickets to Pittsburgh" becomes "pickets to Tittsburgh"
-- and this happens to almost everybody.
Such an abnormal situation is created by centuries of repression. People don't talk about
sex clearly -- they don't talk about sex at all. Even if they talk, they talk in roundabout
ways, they talk diplomatically.
