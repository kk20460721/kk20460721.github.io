CHAPTER 4. DIVE A LITTLE DEEP
After many attempts, Gilbert Goldditch ﬁnally manages to get Gorgeous Gloria to go to his apartment
with him. After a few drinks, Gilbert puts on some soft music, and they settle down on the sofa.
A few minutes later Gloria says, ”You know, Gilbert, you are the ﬁrst man I have met whose kisses
make me sit up and open my eyes.”
”Really?” says Gilbert, happily.
”Yes,” replies Gloria. ”Usually they have the opposite effect!”
Mikhail Gorbachev gets up in the morning and goes out onto his balcony to get some fresh air. The
sun is rising. ”Good morning, red sun!” he exclaims.
”Long live Mikhail Gorbachev!” the sun replies.
Very happy with this, Gorbachev goes about his business. After a busy morning he goes out onto
his balcony again, and sees the sun at its height.
”Good afternoon, sun!” he shouts out.
”Long live Comrade Gorbachev, General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!”
replies the sun.
Very pleased, Gorbachev returns to his work.
That evening, after a hard day, he comes out once again onto his favorite balcony. He sees the sun
setting, and with a smile cries out, ”Good evening, my little sun!”
”I am in the West now,” replies the sun, ”so fuck you!”
