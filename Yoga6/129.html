Question 10
WHERE DO YOUR WORDS COME FROM AND HOW DO YOU RELATE TO
THEM?
There is nobody to relate to them. They come out of the blue -- nobody is
managing. I am not there to manage them. You ask a question and out of the blue
comes the response. They are not my words. The question is yours; the answer is
not mine. The question comes from your mind; the answer is not coming from
any mind. The mind is being used to deliver it, but it is not coming from there.
The medium is not the source.
