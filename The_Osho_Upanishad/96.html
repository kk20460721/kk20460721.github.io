BELOVED OSHO,
WHAT ARE THE ROOTS AND WHAT ARE THE WINGS OF A MEDITATOR?
Meditation is a way of settling in oneself, at the innermost core of your being. Once you have found
the center of your existence, you will have found both the roots and the wings.
The roots are in existence, making you a more integrated human being, an individual. And the wings
are in the fragrance that is released by being in contact with existence. The fragrance consists of
freedom, love, compassion, authenticity, sincerity, a sense of humor, and a tremendous feeling of
blissfulness.
The roots make you an individual, and the wings give you the freedom to love, to be creative, to
share unconditionally the joy that you have found.
The roots and wings come together.
