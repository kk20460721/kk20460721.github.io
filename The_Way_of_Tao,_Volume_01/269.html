MAY NOT THE SPACE BETWEEN HEAVEN AND EARTH
BE COMPARED TO A BELLOWS?
IT IS EMPTIED, YET IT LOSES NOT ITS POWER;
IT IS MOVED AGAIN, AND SENDS FORTH AIR THE MORE.
MUCH SPEECH TO SWIFT EXHAUSTION LEAD WE SEE;
YOUR INNER BEING GUARD, AND KEEP IT FREE.
Life is constructed on many intra-contradictory bases.
Life is not what it appears.
Behind the
apparent elements are elements that are not visible and which are absolutely contrary.
But we never think of the opposite.
When we look at birth, we get no hint of death. If any person talks of death when a birth has taken
place, we brand him as an insane. But death is always hidden behind birth and he who knows, sees
death at once with birth.
