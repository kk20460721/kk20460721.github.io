The hostile is a simple person. You can always trust an angry person, remember. Never trust a

The second kind is more difﬁcult because the sleep of the second kind has more sweet dreams in
it. The ﬁrst kind is suffering from nightmares. Remember, when you suffer from a nightmare there is
every possibility for you to awake. It always happens. When you really go into a nightmare and when
the nightmare becomes too much, you are suddenly awake, your sleep is broken. The nightmare
itself automatically works against the sleep. But when you are sleeping a sweet sleep and you are
seeing beautiful dreams of paradise and heaven, then there is no problem – the sleep can continue.
A sweet dream defends the sleep, a nightmare is against the sleep.
So the second kind, the respectable person, is the least religious person in the world. And, naturally,
he is very cold. He cannot afford to he hot. These are the people who, if they come to this ashram,
they immediately escape.
