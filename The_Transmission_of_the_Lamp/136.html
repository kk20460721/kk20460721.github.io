Question 4
BELOVED OSHO,
YOU ONCE SAID THAT WHILE WE SIT WITH YOU AND THE MORE WE BECOME RELAXED,
WITHOUT OUR KNOWING, OUR UNCONSCIOUS WILL START EMERGING LIKE STEAM FROM
A BOILING TEAPOT. AS THE UNCONSCIOUS STARTS BOILING UP, AND ALL THE SKELETONS
AND DRAGONS START COMING OUT OF THE DARKNESS, I FEEL I NEED SOME GUIDANCE
ON HOW TO DEAL WITH IT – FOR MYSELF, AND IN RELATION TO OTHER PEOPLE.
CAN YOU PLEASE COMMENT?
First, don’t do anything about it – just watch. Doing will create problems. You will get mixed up by it;
your emotions, your sentiments, your thoughts, may get mixed with it. Don’t do anything; just remain
aloof and watch.
Just by watching it will slowly disappear like smoke disappearing into the sky. You don’t have to do
anything at all.
