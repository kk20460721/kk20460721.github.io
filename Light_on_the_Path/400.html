Question 1
BELOVED OSHO,
WHY DO I ALLOW HABIT TO DOMINATE CONSCIOUSNESS?
Almost everybody does it, unless one becomes enlightened.
The habit is the easier way to do a thing. The whole mechanism of a habit is that you need not be
conscious about it. It has become a mechanical part of you, a part of your robot mind: it does things
by itself. You can go on thinking other things, dreaming other dreams, and the habit takes every
action to its end – with more perfection because a habit is mechanical, and machines don’t commit
mistakes.
So everybody is dominated by habit. And it is one of the most important things, to get out of this
domination. It is moving from mechanicalness to consciousness.
In the beginning it is very arduous.
You are doing one thing, and at the same time you have to
remain conscious – so you are divided. Because of your division, your action may not be as perfect
as when the habit does it alone; it simply knows how to do it.
