My concept of a future therapy is a community – ﬁve hundred or one thousand people, two hundred
families living together in a therapeutic climate. Then just as you put an individual in a therapy group,
you can put a family into a community. Whenever one person goes mad or goes berserk, bring the
whole family into the community. And that will be the real treatment.
Things are very much interconnected, and the emphasis should go from the individual to the group,
and from the group to the community, from the community to the society, from the society to the
whole planet earth.
