A parable:
THE GREAT MYSTIC, Rabia of Basra, was immensely beautiful. And a beauty not of this world.
Once a rich young man from Iran comes to Basra. He asks people, ”Is there anything that is out of
the way, something special here?”
”Yes,” they all tell him. ”We have the most beautiful woman of the world!”
The young man naturally becomes interested and he asks, ”Where can I ﬁnd her?”
And they all laugh and say, ”Well, where else?... in a brothel!”
That repulses the rich young man, but ﬁnally he decides to go. And when he gets there, the matron
asks for an exorbitant fee. He pays the fee and is ushered in. There, in a silent and simple room,
a ﬁgure is praying. What beauty she has! He has never seen such beauty and grace, not even in
