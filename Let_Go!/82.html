[A sannyasin has separated from his girlfriend of two years. Osho says it is a good thing to get out
of a relationship when it has become stale.
The sannyasin says: The hard thing for me is to accept that I’m all right. And Osho answers: That is
the most difﬁcult thing for everybody, you reply, nobody can accept that. That’s my whole effort, the
whole refrain, to tell you again and again that you are perfectly all right. For a moment you listen but
you don’t believe it. Or even if for a moment you start believing it, the old mind comes in and says
’How can you be all right?’ And you start putting interpretations on my statement. But I am simply
saying that you are all right with no conditions attached.
The sannyasin says: But this voice keeps coming back again and again. And Osho tells him: That
too will go slowly, slowly. How long can it continue to come? That is the struggle between the master
and the disciple, it is a constant wrestling. Hopefully you will be defeated!]
