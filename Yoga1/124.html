CHAPTER 8. STOP, AND IT IS HERE!
milieu – happy, thankful. What has happened? We can be more happy than anyone, but we have
lost contact with existence.
And that existence is here and now, and an impatient mind cannot be in contact with it. Impatience
is like a feverish, mad state of mind; you go on running. Even if the goal comes, you cannot stand
there because the running has become just the habit. Even if you reach the goal you will miss it,
you will pass it because you cannot stop. If you can stop, the goal is not to be searched.
Zen Master Hui-Hai, has said that, ”Seek, and you will lose; don’t seek, and you can get it
immediately. Stop, and it is here. Run, it is nowhere.”
