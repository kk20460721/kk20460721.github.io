CHAPTER 6. THE PASSION FOR THE IMPOSSIBLE
WHAT IS WRONG IN STUDYING RELIGIOUS SCRIPTURES?
Who will be studying? You will be studying. When you read a statement of Jesus, who will interpret
it to you? You will interpret it. The statement will no longer be Jesus’, it will become yours. You will
have pulled it down to your level.
How can you read scriptures unless you attain to that consciousness out of which the j ﬂowed? If
you want to understand Krishna, his Geeta, you will have to attain to Krishna consciousness. There
is no other way, otherwise you will corrupt the scripture. when I say ’Please burn the scriptures’ I am
not against the scriptures, I am trying to save them, otherwise you will corrupt them. If you want to
understand Jesus you will have to attain to Christ consciousness – at least a little bit. When a little
window opens in your heart that gives you a vision of the sky that was fully available to Jesus – only
then. Otherwise you will go on interpreting in your own way.
That’s what is happening always.
