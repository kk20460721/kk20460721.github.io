CHAPTER 18.
There is a great message in your name – that now is the only time there is and love is the only god
there is. So worship the god of love... and now! This very moment be loving; don’t postpone. Either
now or never. If you choose some other time, you have chosen never. One has to love intensely,
passionately.
And this is the only time there is. So sing, dance, celebrate, but now. When the past disappears
and the future too, and you live only in the now, life burns bright, it becomes luminous. Then one is
aﬂame. Then there is the grace of god, and great miracles start happening.
