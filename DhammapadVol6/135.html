Lao Tzu would laugh; Jesus wept. Now it is for you to choose. I love both the men; in
fact laughing and weeping are two sides of the same coin. And because of this story that
Jesus wept, I say something which Christians have denied down the ages. Christians
have been saying that Jesus never laughed. Now a man who is capable of weeping is
bound to be capable of laughing, it is impossible to weep if you cannot laugh. In fact
laughter and weeping are not opposites, but complementaries -- two extremes of the
same spectrum.
