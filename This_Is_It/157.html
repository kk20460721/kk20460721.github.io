Try tonight and you will know what it is... because it cannot be explained. No need to smile with the
lips on the face but just as if you are smiling from the belly; the belly is smiling. And it is a smile, not
a laughter, so it is very very soft, delicate, fragile – like a small rose ﬂower opening in the belly and
the fragrance spreading all over the body.
Once you have known what this smile is you can remain happy for twenty-four hours. And whenever
you feel that you are missing that happiness, just close your eyes and catch hold of that smile again
and it will be there. And in the daytime as many times you want you can catch hold of it. It is always
there, mm? Good!
