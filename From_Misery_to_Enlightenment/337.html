CHAPTER 21. MY MESSAGE – A MATTER OF LIFE AND DEATH
In listening you have taken the ﬁrst step towards being reborn.
So it is not a philosophy that you can just make an overcoat of and go bragging about.
It is not a doctrine in which you can ﬁnd consolation about harassing questions; a doctrine which
can make those questions subdued, and slowly slowly forgotten.
No, my message is not some verbal communication.
It is far more risky.
It is nothing less than death and rebirth.
