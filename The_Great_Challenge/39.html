25 October 1970 pm
Question 1

GENUINE EXPERIENCES OF SAMADHI? IS PERMANENT SELF-REALIZATION POSSIBLE
THROUGH THE USE OF LSD? IS THERE ANY HARM IN USING LSD AS AN AID TO
MEDITATION?

Everything in the unconscious can be projected, but if you have not known anything about kundalini
or samadhi beforehand it is impossible to feel them through LSD.
