The fourth question:
Question 4
BELOVED MASTER,
WHERE DO REBELLION AND SURRENDER MEET?
Prem Rajo, rebellion and surrender meet in the idea of the ego. Drop the ego and
simultaneously surrender happens, and rebellion too.
I know what you mean by your question. You mean that rebellion and surrender seem
to be polar opposites -- how can they meet? How can one be rebellious AND
surrendered? That's your question. That's how mind thinks about rebellion and
surrender; through the mind you cannot see them meeting anywhere.
A man who is surrendered will look nonrebellious. A man who is rebellious will always
be disobeying -- how can he surrender? He may die, but he will not surrender.
You know only one kind of surrender: the surrender that is forced on you, the
surrender that is not done by you but you are made to do it -- at the point of a dagger
you are forced to surrender. That is not the surrender I am talking about.
