When you are sitting alone and you feel sick, accept it This is part of it. The old house is crumbling,
collapsing, and the new house is not yet on the horizon. You cannot see anything of it because you
are the old house, so you will crumble and disappear, and in your disappearance arises something
which is completely discontinuous with your past. That is the new being, the spiritual being. Then
one can live again.
