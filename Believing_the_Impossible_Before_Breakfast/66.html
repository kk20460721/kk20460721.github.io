What name you use for god – god, tao, nirvana – doesn’t matter; if you really want to know you
will have to become utterly silent. That is the meaning of your name, Satyamurti; you will have to
reﬂect reality, only then will you know. I can help you to cleanse your mirror but I will not give you
any answer about what god is.
When your mirror is clean you will know.
