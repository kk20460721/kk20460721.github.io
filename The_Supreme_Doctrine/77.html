CHAPTER 6. GOD IS EXISTENCE
thought has been invited, and now it comes and still you give energy to it. You can give energy in
two ways. If you are against it you will also give energy. In both ways the thought will feed upon it.
There is only one way to be disconnected, and that is to be indifferent.
Buddha has called it
UPEKSHA. He said if you are indifferent to the process of thoughts, they will disappear.
Insist on being indifferent. Do not take any attitude, do not choose. Just remain a witness, and they
will disappear. And when they disappear, suddenly the worshipper is revealed: suddenly you are
revealed to yourself. That revelation alone is Brahman, and NOT THIS THAT PEOPLE WORSHIP
HERE.
