8
Inscape – the ultimate annihilation
7 April 1989 pm in Gautam the Buddha Auditorium
BELOVED OSHO,
A MONK ASKED DAITEN, A DISCIPLE OF SEKITO, ”HOW IS IT WHEN ONE MEETS THE
PERSON-IN-THERE?”
DAITEN REPLIED, ”THE PERSON IS NOT IN THERE ANYMORE.”
THE MONK ASKED, ”WHAT IS ‘IN THERE’?”
DAITEN SAID, ”DON’T ASK THAT QUESTION.”
THE MONK THEN ASKED, ”IN THE OCEAN OF MISERY, THE WAVES ARE DEEP. WITH WHAT
CAN WE MAKE A BOAT?”
DAITEN REPLIED, ”MAKE A BOAT WITH WOOD.”
THE MONK SAID, ”IF WE DO, CAN WE GO ACROSS THE OCEAN?”
DAITEN REPLIED, ”THE BLIND ARE STILL BLIND; THE DUMB ARE STILL DUMB.”
ON ANOTHER OCCASION A MONK FROM KOREA CAME TO SEE DAITEN. WHEN THE MONK
UNROLLED THE SITTING MAT TO MAKE A BOW, DAITEN SAID, ”BEFORE YOU LEAVE YOUR
COUNTRY, GET THE SINGLE PHRASE!”
