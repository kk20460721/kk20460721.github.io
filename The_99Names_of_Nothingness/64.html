During these two years that you are in the Middle East, try this: always the ﬁrst thing to decide is, is
this a heart matter or a mind matter? And in life there are almost ninety-nine percent mind matters,
rarely a heart matter. But that one percent is more valuable than the ninety-nine percent. And if
there is a question of whether to save the ninety-nine percent or to save the one percent, I will say
’Save the one percent and lose the ninety-nine percent.’ But ordinarily there is no question like that.
Use your mind more, continue to meditate, and things will become more and more clear.
