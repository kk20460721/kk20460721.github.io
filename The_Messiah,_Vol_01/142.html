If Rajiv Gandhi really wants the country to become a little wise, the beginning should be from the

They should change the whole bureaucracy which we have inherited from the British imperialists.
This bureaucracy was not created to give people freedom, to give people freedom of expression.
This bureaucracy was created just for opposite reasons: to destroy people, their freedom, their
individuality, to destroy all kinds of intelligence. And the same bureaucracy continues.
If Rajiv Gandhi really means what he is saying, he should start with his own government. It is full of
all kinds of idiots. Their whole function is not to allow the country to grow.
