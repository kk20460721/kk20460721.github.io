Question 6
I WANT TO PROPAGATE AGAINST YOU. WOULD YOU PLEASE BLESS ME?
This is a good idea. I bless you absolutely; because to propagate against me is
also to propagate for me. Knowingly, unknowingly, whosoever says anything
against me says something about me, and nobody knows: if you are talking
against me to somebody, he may become interested in me. So go and propagate
with all my blessings.
