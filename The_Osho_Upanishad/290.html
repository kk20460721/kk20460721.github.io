CHAPTER 23. ‘NOTHING’ IS MY SWORD
But such minute concern, and a decisiveness to do something to its perfection – the same thing can
be turned into any dimension. If it becomes spirituality, it will be the same – the same quality, the
same determination, the same devotion.
So if you are feeling relaxed, it is not against action. One has to understand that relaxation is the
very source of energy; it can be converted into action. In the night you go to sleep so that in the
morning you are rejuvenated. The night has not been a wastage, the night has helped you to recover
the energy that you have used before. Again you are young, again fresh.
In meditation, whatever happens is bound to be expressed in creativity.
What you are doing is
a creative act, and for your creative act you have renounced everything. For your love you have
renounced everything. It will be appreciated by people when they come to know of it.
