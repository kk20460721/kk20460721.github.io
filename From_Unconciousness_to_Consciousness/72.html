He said, ”I know. Three others are already here. This is a madhouse.”
The whole night Winston Churchill had to remain there. He asked again and again, ”Let me phone

But the man said, ”They all say that they are REALLY Winston Churchill, and they all want to phone
to higher authorities. Who do we listen to? You four can discuss and argue and decide.”
The whole night Winston Churchill had to live with three other Winston Churchills, who were
absolutely, as absolutely certain as he was. Even he became suspicious: ”Perhaps I have gone
mad, perhaps these people are right.”
