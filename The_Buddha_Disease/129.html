CHAPTER 25. LIFE IS A RISK, THAT’S WHY IT IS SO BEAUTIFUL
Drop the jealousy rather than dropping love. The fear comes because of jealousy. The fear comes
because of the ego – the other may reject you:
then drop the ego!
Rather than throwing the
bathwater, you are keeping the bathwater and throwing the baby!
Drop ego, drop jealousy, and
take the risk.
Life is a risk – that’s why it is so beautiful, of value, because it is a risk. It is an adventure. Good!
