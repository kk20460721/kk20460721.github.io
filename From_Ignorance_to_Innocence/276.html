CHAPTER 19. RELIGION IS REBELLION
It has nothing to do with me.
It is simply religion.
You have to understand, as totally as possible, that just a pure religion has more possibility of
surviving, because then you don’t put any boundaries on it.
I have not put any boundaries on it. And I don’t want to put any boundaries on you: of discipline, of
morality, of virtue.
I have given you freedom, and I have given you individuality, and I have given you just a little taste
of something that is always yours.
Just you have to claim it.
