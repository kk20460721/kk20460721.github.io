That’s what sannyas is all about: risking the known for the unknown, the familiar for the unfamiliar,
risking that which you have for that which you don’t have – not only do you not have it, but it may not
exist at all. Who knows? There is no guarantee.
But blessed are those who can take such a risk, who can gamble such a gamble.
Their life
becomes the life of insecurity – but anything that ever grows, only grows in insecurity.
Anything
that ever integrates, crystallises, crystallises only when impossible challenges are accepted – not
only accepted, but welcomed.
