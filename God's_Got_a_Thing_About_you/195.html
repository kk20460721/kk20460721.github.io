CHAPTER 29.
Love is the only alchemy that transforms a man into something superhuman. And my work is nothing
but creating a situation in which your love can start ﬂowing. It is there, frozen; it needs a little warmth,
a provocation, and then it starts ﬂowing. It needs to be called forth, and that is the function of the
Master: to call forth the intrinsic in you, the potential in you, and to help it become actual.
God is everybody’s potentiality; it has to become everybody’s actuality too. Unless it happens we
remain in misery. And when it happens there is ecstasy, unbounded ecstasy. And once it starts
happening it goes on happening; then there is no end to it. Yes, it begins but it never ends... it has
a beginning but no end.
