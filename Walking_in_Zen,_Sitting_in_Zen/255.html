Maria was a student of sociology. For her research paper she decided to make a trip into the interior
of Brazil. Caught in a tropical downpour she lost her way in the jungle. Finally two men found her,
drenched to the skin.
”You’ll never ﬁnd your way out in this storm,” said one.
”The river is ﬂooded.
You’re welcome to
spend the night at our place.”
Maria felt relieved and happy to have an opportunity to experience the authentic lives of some people
of the interior. Once at the house, the men prepared a meal for her and afterwards played the guitar
and sang folksongs. When they grew tired, the Brazilian informed her that there was only one bed.
”That’s okay,” she said, ”I can sleep in bed with the two of you!”
The men were reluctant, but ﬁnally agreed.
After the three tossed and turned for a while, they
decided to give in and make love. Maria gave each man a condom and then the three of them had

The next morning Maria thanked the two men for their hospitality and went on her way back home
