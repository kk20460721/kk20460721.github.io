WHY DO MAHAVIR, BUDDHA, AND RAJNEESH NOT SING AND DANCE
PHYSICALLY?
They are doing so all the time, but you need some deeper eyes to see it.
You never ask the real question. People go on asking, "Where is God hidden?"
They never ask whether their eyes are open or not. They ask, "Where should I
seek him?" They never ask, "How should I become available to him so that he can
seek me?"
You ask why Buddha, Mahavir, Rajneesh are not dancing. They are dancing all
the time. Their whole being is a dance, but you need some other eyes to see it.
You don't have the right eyes. Create them.
