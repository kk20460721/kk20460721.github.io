CHAPTER 12.
You will never ﬁnd any relationship between you and your name; there is none! You can be called ’a’,
you can be called ’b’, you can be called ’c’... it will not make any difference to you; you will remain
the same, called by ’a’, called by ’b’, you will remain the same. A rose is a rose is a rose... you can
call it by any name. You don’t have any name.
You have to ﬁnd some relevance with me, not with the name. And that is happening. And I am happy
– it is happening every day: you are coming closer and closer.
The Tongue-Tip Taste of Tao
