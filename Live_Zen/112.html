Randy Musthaver picks up Polly, the prostitute, on the street and asks her, ”How much?”
”Ten dollars,” she replies, ”unless you want it Spanish style, but then it is ten dollars extra.”
Randy goes for the Spanish style, so they go up to Polly’s room and have a great time.
When they are ﬁnished, Randy gets dressed and is heading for the door, when he stops and says,
”Well, I really enjoyed that, but tell me, what was Spanish about it?”
”My God,” says Polly, ”I nearly forgot!”
She then stands up on the bed, raises one arm, snaps her ﬁngers and shouts, ”Ole!”
On a fullmoon evening, Count Dracula walks into a men’s store and asks to see some ties.
When the salesgirl looks into his eyes, she becomes hypnotized. Dracula sinks his sharp teeth into
her throat and takes a deep drink.
As she wakes up, the girl remembers nothing, and Count Dracula buys a silk tie and leaves the
store.
The next night he is back.
”Would you like to try something different tonight?” asks the girl, innocently.
”No,” chuckles the vampire, ”I want something in exactly the same vein!”
