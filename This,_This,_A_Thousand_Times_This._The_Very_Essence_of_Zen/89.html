Olga Omsky, a Russian housewife, is the envy of all her neighbors, because she always has a
plentiful supply of fresh vegetables and fruit.
One day, one of her neighbors is visiting her.
”Tell me, comrade Olga,” asks the neighbor, ”how do you manage it?”
”It is quite simple,” explains Olga, ”I have a parrot which I have trained to speak. Whenever I go
to the market, the parrot sits on the handle of my shopping cart. I leave the cart in the middle of
the market, and when the parrot starts squawking, ‘Long live communism!’ everyone throws at it
whatever they can get their hands on!”
Paddy gets a new job, and on the ﬁrst day, the boss walks up to him and says, ”What is your name?”
”Patrick Murphy!” Paddy replies.
”Look here,” snaps the boss, ”say ‘Sir’, when you speak to me!”
”All right,” says Paddy, ”Sir Patrick Murphy!”
