The word god is not God, because the ultimate cannot have a name.
It is nameless – because
names are given by others. A child is born. The child is born nameless, then a name is to be given.
That name doesn’t come from the inner source of the child’s consciousness. It comes from without.
It is a label – useful, utilitarian, but artiﬁcial. The child will become a victim. He will identify himself
with this name which is given, which really doesn’t belong to him.
