To make a Polack feel insecure, invite him home to play with the dog.

skis?”

a tree. A man passing by watches this for a moment and says, ”Why don’t you pick some apples
and put them in a trough? It will save you time.”
”Don’t be a dummy,” snorts Kowalski. ”Pigs don’t care about time.”
