CHAPTER 24. DOUBT OR FAITH, LIFE OR DEATH; THE BASES OF DIFFERENT PATHS
Death can be used for deep meditation because then you become inactive. Energy is released from
the world; it can move inwards. That is why a death-like posture is suggested. Use life, use death,
for discovering that which is beyond both.
