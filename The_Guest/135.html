But don’t miss this opportunity. This time, die consciously. But you have to start right now to live
consciously; only then can you die consciously: Even if you can live consciously for a few years,
that will be enough.
Even a few months or even a few days and if intensity is great even a few
minutes are enough to live consciously; then one becomes capable of dying consciously. And to die
consciously is to be resurrected into a totally different dimension, the dimension of the divine.
I would like all of my sannyasins to die so deeply that they are never born again, so that they can
disappear into the cosmos, become part of the whole.
