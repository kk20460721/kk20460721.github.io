In fact they are very helpful. They function as a lubricating agent, otherwise you will
be struggling with everybody and life will become just a constant war.
You have walked on somebody’s toe and you say, ’Sorry.’
You don’t mean it – not even a single
thought has crossed your mind of being sorry.
You simply say, ’Sorry.’
It is lubricating.

You are not alone.
And it is good to learn the
mannerisms – but do it deliberately.
Act authentically – that’s what I would like to say to you. When acting, act authentically, that’s all.
