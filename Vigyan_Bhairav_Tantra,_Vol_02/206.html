The whole emphasis has to go from the ﬁlled spaces to the unﬁlled spaces.
You are reading a
book. There are words, there are sentences, but between the words there are gaps, between the
sentences there are gaps. In those gaps you are. The whiteness of the paper you are, and the
black dots are just clouds of thought and desire moving on you. Change the emphasis, change the
gestalt. Don’t look at the black dots. Look at the white.
In your inner being, look at the gaps. Be indifferent to the ﬁlled spaces, the occupied spaces. Be
interested in the gaps, the intervals.
Through those intervals you can dissolve into the ultimate
beauty.
