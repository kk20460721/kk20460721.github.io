TATRA DHYANAJAM ANASAYAM.
ONLY THE ORIGINAL MIND which is born of meditation is free from desires: this is one of the most
signiﬁcant sutras.
FIRST, WHAT IS THE ORIGINAL MIND? – because the original mind is the very goal of all yogas.
The East has been searching continuously the path to the original mind. The original mind is that
mind which you had before you were born, not in this life, but before you entered the world of
desires; before you were conﬁned to thoughts, desires, instincts, body, mind; that original space,
uncontaminated by anything; that original sky, unclouded – that’s the original mind.
