CHAPTER
8
Dying in Wonder
18 December 1978 am in Buddha Hall
The ﬁrst question:
BELOVED MASTER,
Question 1
YOU APPEAR TO HAVE MADE A POINT OF ATTACKING THE PRIME MINISTER, MORARJI
DESAI, AND BACKING INDIRA GANDHI, THE FORMER PRIME MINISTER, WITH ALL THE
SPIRITUAL POWER AT YOUR COMMAND. WHAT IS THE BACKGROUND? DO YOU WISH TO
REPLACE MR. DESAI WITH MRS. GANDHI? IN ANY CASE, IS SUCH AN INTERVENTION IN
INDIAN POLITICAL AFFAIRS BY A SPIRITUAL PERSON JUSTIFIED?
R.K. Karanjia,
Editor, BLITZ.
My dear Karanjia, I am not personally against Morarjibhai Desai.
But I am against the rotten
mentality that he represents, I am against the Hindu chauvinistic mind that he has. I am against
his obscurantist attitudes, and approaches. I am against his stubbornness in imposing his personal
fads upon the whole country. I am against his bureaucratic, autocratic, dictatorial methods. I am
against his unscientiﬁc attitudes towards life. I have nothing to do with Morarjibhai Desai, but all
these things combined together are a calamity for the country.
