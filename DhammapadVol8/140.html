Question 7

SHOULD ONE TRY TO BE RICH OR NOT?
Asango, meditate on Murphy's maxim: Don't care if you are rich or not as long as you
can live comfortably and have everything you want.
That's exactly what I have been doing and that's exactly what I would like you to do.
Why bother whether you are rich or not? In fact, people go into unnecessary worries.
Whatsoever you have, enjoy it -- it is already too much. You cannot look at it because
your mind is constantly occupied with doing this, becoming that. And all that existence
goes on giving you, you go on neglecting. You never even thank existence for it; you
don't have any gratitude. Otherwise, even if you don't possess anything, you can live a
very rich life.
