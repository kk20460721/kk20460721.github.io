I WONDER – WHAT WOULD THE WORLD BE LIKE WITHOUT ART AND THE CREATIVE

me, because to understand me you will have to drop your memory. Your memory interferes. You

according to your past. You cannot understand me if you are not herenow... only then the meeting.
Only in that moment you ARE with me; otherwise, you are physically present here, psychologically
absent.
