And a staff – one wonders why a staff. It is because of the Indian dogs. There are so many, and they
are particularly against all kinds of uniformed people. Although the monk’s is not much of a uniform,
still it is a uniform because all the Hindu monks are the same, with their begging bowls, that mini
mini underwear, the staff – and all naked.
Dogs feel suspicious of uniforms; they think that something somewhere is wrong. And perhaps they
are right, because the army uses uniforms, the police use uniforms, postmen use uniforms – and
the monks use uniforms. It seems all the wrong kinds of people are in uniforms. So the staff is
absolutely necessary to keep the dogs away.
