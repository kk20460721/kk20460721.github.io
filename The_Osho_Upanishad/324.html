CHAPTER 26. THE MASTER – YOUR DEATH AND YOUR RESURRECTION
You are just a dewdrop.
And as you meditate, the dewdrop starts slipping from the petals of the lotus towards the ocean.
When the meditation is complete, the dewdrop has disappeared into the ocean.
Or you can say, the ocean has disappeared into the dewdrop – it is the same.
The Osho Upanishad
