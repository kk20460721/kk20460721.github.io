CHAPTER 5. FREEDOM IS MY GOD
What a great and beautiful answer! Because even Buddha is a thought in your mind. Even Buddha
has said, ”If I come on the path in your meditation, just cut my head off immediately, remove me.”
That’s what Baso is saying: Buddha is mind, no-mind is the way.
THE MONK THEN ASKED, ”ARE THE BUDDHA AND THE WAY SOMEWHAT DIFFERENT?”
BASO REPLIED, ”THE BUDDHA IS LIKE STRETCHING OUT THE HAND, THE WAY IS LIKE
CLENCHING THE FIST.”
Opening the hand or closing the hand are not two things, although they appear as two. Just let your
mind be silent and settled and you have entered into no-mind.
There is no difference. The difference is only of an open hand and a ﬁst. Not much of a difference,
not a difference that can be called difference. And yet, apparently it is different. But only apparently.
