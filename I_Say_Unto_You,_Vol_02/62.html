CHAPTER 3. TO THE NEW AS THE NEW
Before Jesus was, I am.
Abraham participated in Christ-consciousness and disappeared. So Jesus participated in Christ-
consciousness and disappeared. Now there is no question of time, now time exists not. Now there
is no more any time and no more any space. This state is what Buddha calls NIRVANA, and Jesus
calls the ’kingdom of God’.
Meditate on these sutras. They are incredible. Go into them, and you will be immensely beneﬁtted.
Great will be your grace if you can understand them.
