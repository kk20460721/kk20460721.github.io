CHAPTER 13. TRANSCENDENCE THROUGH BEING
The man who asked felt that he was still unconscious, because that man had escaped. And how
can that man who was beating him serve him now to make him conscious? He was fanning him, the
man said, ”Ali, you seem to be still unconscious, confused. This is not that man.”
Ali said, ”How can He not be That? I cannot see anything except That. So when He was beating me
I knew who He was, and now that He is serving me I know who He is – but they are both the same!”
This is a non-dualistic concept, feeling. When you know That, this disappears; when you know this,
That remains just a concept somewhere. But start from yourself Don’t go to ﬁnd it out anywhere
else; otherwise the journey will be very long. And you may reach, you may not reach. Take a total
about-turn – seek it in your own center.
