Just be conscious. Whatsoever you are doing – wearing a mask – be conscious; wear it knowingly.
It should not be an automatic thing.
[The sannyasin adds: I feel many fears with this also. Whenever I change masks there is fear.]
Fear is there too, and that too, is natural.
Death is there so fear is bound to be there.
You will
become fearless only when you have come to know that which is deathless within you, not before it.
Accept that too, and be aware of it. One should be aware of everything layer by layer, mm? Man is
just like an onion. Layer upon layer – fear, jealousy, anger, hatred, sadness – then you come to an
emptiness. That emptiness upon which there are so many layers is you, your being. The taste of
being is just like non-being. It is so vast that it is empty.
This is good that layers are coming on the way. There is no need to throw them, no need to run
naked on the streets. You know that you are naked under the clothes; perfectly okay, one has to
remember that.
But you have to wear clothes because everybody may not be ready to see you
naked, so why unnecessarily impose on them?
