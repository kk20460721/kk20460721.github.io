Kaivalya Upanishad
29 March 1972 pm at Mt Abu Meditation Camp, India
WISE MEN MAKE THEIR CONSCIENCE THE LOWER
ARONY, THE CHURNING STICK, AND AUM, THE UPPER
CHURNING STICK.
AND WITH THE HELP OF THESE TWO, THEY
PRACTICE THE CHURNING OF KNOWLEDGE.
IN THE FIRE OF KNOWLEDGE, BORN OUT OF
THIS FRICTION, THEY BURN ALL THEIR BLEMISHES AND
BECOME FREE.
