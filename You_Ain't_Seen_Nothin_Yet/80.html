CHAPTER 19.
Don’t be worried about your parents! They will be happy, very happy. Just go and dance and sing
and let them know how crazy you have become!
[A sannyasin says: I had a strange two weeks in Germany and I don’t know what happened to me.
I really felt ’Oh, I’m going to be mad; I can’t control myself.’]
You have come to the right place! You’re going mad – nothing to be worried about! But here we
do madness with a method. Don’t be worried: madness is the highest form of sanity; just wait and
experience!
