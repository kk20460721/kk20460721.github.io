Fitzgerald went to a bar for ﬁve consecutive nights and watched Manzini sitting over in a corner
booth. It was an incredible sight. Good-looking, wildly-shaped girls, alone or in groups of two or
three, would wander in and soon make their way to the weird-looking Manzini.
”I don’t understand it,” grumbled Fitzgerald to the bartender. ”I don’t see how the Eye-talian does it.”
”Me either,” says the barkeep. ”I been watching him for weeks. He certainly ain’t handsome, he is a
