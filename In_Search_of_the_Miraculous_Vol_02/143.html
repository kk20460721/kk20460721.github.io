Krishnamurti. Great efforts were made with him to ﬁx a peg, but they failed. The whole school of

persons had a hand in this effort. There were also the hands of higher souls behind this – of persons

tirthankaras, but the opportunity passed away without bearing fruit. The peg could not be ﬁxed, and
so the world lost the beneﬁt of a tirthankara which could have been possible through Krishnamurti.
