CHAPTER 29. TRUTH – NOT A HIGHWAY BUT A HILL TRACK
But the guide is nobody other than Bodhidharma; hence, laughter is our most essential quality. And
a sense of humor, playfulness, fun, is something sacred – nothing profane in it. Just listen to it.
I don’t laugh, for the simple reason that if I laugh, you will stop laughing immediately. To allow you
to laugh, I have to contain myself. I laugh in my room when I am alone!
