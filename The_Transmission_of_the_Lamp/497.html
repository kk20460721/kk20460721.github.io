CHAPTER 46. ENERGY MOVES BETTER WHEN IT IS WARMER
Mind is part of the society, of the whole human heritage.
And the watcher is your own individuality.
This is a real trinity. There is no question of man or woman because the witness is neither man
nor woman. The body will go back to nature as you become awakened, mind will disperse back to
the society as you become awakened, and the witness will go back to what I call the godliness that
surrounds the whole existence.
But the Christian trinity is ugly. You can create a trinity for meditators which is far more real and far
more useful.
The body has to be taken care of, has to be respected, has to be loved. It is your home.
