Latifa, it is not just your problem, it is the problem of the whole German race; and the way out of it is
not to get identiﬁed with it.
Nobody is superior.
Nobody is inferior.
Everybody is unique.
Because there are not two persons who are the same, you cannot compare marigolds with roses:
they are two different ﬂowers. You cannot compare two human beings with each other, they are two
different ﬂowers. But this is not a question of intellectual conviction, it is a question of being aware,
and destroying the identiﬁcation.
Don’t try to be superior, just try to be yourself. You are not inferior, but, remember it: nobody else is
inferior. You are not superior.
Aristotle has created a difﬁculty for human beings. He created one of the basic troubles in the human
mind, because he knew only: either you are superior, or you are inferior. In everything it was either,
