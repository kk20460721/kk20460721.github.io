years old.
The difﬁculty for the missionaries was that in RIGVEDA there is a description of a certain
constellation of stars which happened, according to astronomers, exactly ninety thousand years
ago. Since then that constellation has not happened. So one thing is certain: whoever was writing it
was an astronomer, and what he was writing was present to him – the constellation, a certain state
of stars which has not happened again.
