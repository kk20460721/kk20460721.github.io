In this non-duality, in this meaningless splendor of existence, in this state of unconcern --
not waiting for anything, not desiring for anything -- you become so innocent, so open and so
vulnerable, that the greatest experience simply arises in you. You have prepared the ground.
Enlightenment is not a goal; it is your intrinsic potentiality. When you are relaxed, the
lotus flower of enlightenment opens its petals. And not only you, but the whole existence
rejoices in this tremendous experience. There is no meaning in it but there is great splendor.
There is no meaning in it, but there is great significance. There is no meaning in it, but there is
great contentment and fulfillment and a feeling that you have come home.
Okay, Maneesha?
Yes, Osho.
