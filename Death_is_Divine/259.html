CHAPTER 9. THOUGHT OF MEMORY-INTELLIGENCE
SHREE GORAKH-YATI SAYS DO THESE AND KNOW YOU’RE AN EMPEROR.
Gorakh says, O emperor! I want to give you the one message of awakening. I want to give you the
one remembrance. Awake! This awakening is within your capacity. It is your potential. Just as a tree
is hidden in the seed, in the same way god is hidden in you.
Besides placement in line what will be gained in the end?
I have never seen a drop that won’t become the ocean.
You are a drop. You can be the ocean. The ocean is hidden in the drop. And until you become the
ocean, don’t accept relief, do not relax. Let that thirst take hold of you like a ﬂaming ﬁre. And all will
be reduced to ash. And all will be burnt up. And all will be destroyed. Then higher life is uncovered.
DIE, O YOGI, DIE! DIE, SWEET IS DYING.
DIE THAT DEATH GORAKH DIED AND SAW.
Enough for today?
