CHAPTER 9. BEYOND THE SHADOW
UNTIL YOU SEE YOURSELF AS A SPECK OF DUST
YOU CANNOT POSSIBLE REACH THAT PLACE;
SELF COULD NEVER BREATHE THAT AIR,
SO WEND YOUR WAY THERE WITHOUT SELF.
You cannot enter into God as a self. You can enter into God only as a no-self – what Buddha calla
ANATTA, no-self.
If you really want God, you have to disappear. This is the problem: people want God, but they don’t
want to disappear. They want to possess God as they possess other properties. Hence they can go
on searching for thousands of lives but they will not ﬁnd God, because they will not fulﬁll the basic
requirement.
This is the basic requirement:
UNSELF YOURSELF...
And immediately God starts showering on you, INSTANTLY. The whole world becomes luminous;
immediately all is celebration. Remember this key of Hakim Sanai. This is a master key: this can
open the doors of all the mysteries.
UNSELF YOURSELF.
