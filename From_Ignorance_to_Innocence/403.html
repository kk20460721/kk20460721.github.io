Now, the pope seems to be not aware that to call the class struggle a sin means you are supporting

man who is asleep. He does not know the implications of his own words, his own actions, because

And yes, it is needed that one day the world becomes classless. And by a classless society I don’t
mean communism. I simply mean enlightened people who can see that there is no need for poverty
to exist; we have enough technology to destroy it. There is no need to destroy the capitalist. All that
is needed is to spread capitalism so that everybody becomes a capitalist.
Now, my approach is just the opposite of communism.
