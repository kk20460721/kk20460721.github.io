He is absolutely in agreement with my idea that nothing should be repressed, everything
should be expressed. If you feel like crying, cry; there is no harm in it, it is absolutely in
accord with nature. If you feel like doing anything that is arising in you with a great urge, do
it, because once done you are freed from it.
If each moment we go on doing everything naturally, we don't accumulate in the
storehouse of consciousness any rubbish, any crap.
And if your consciousness is clean, pure, innocent, the moment is not far away when you
will explode into a luminous splendor. That's what is known as enlightenment, or awakening,
or self-realization.
