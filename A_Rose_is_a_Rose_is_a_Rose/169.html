CHAPTER 26.
There are monasteries in the world which are against the world. They create an alternate world; no
ﬁght, no anger, everybody is polite and everybody is nice, everybody is goody-goody. But everything
is false, everybody is pretending, forcing. No, I am not for that.
I want real people here. And if grace comes, it has to come through being real, not through being
hypocrites, not by pretending.
So thank Asheesh. He is good, he is giving you great chances. So when you go back, tell him.
’Continue, Asheesh,’ mm?
[The sannyasin answers: He can be very heavy.]
He can be, but you have to enjoy it. He’s a great taskmaster! You have to enjoy it. Drop your ego.
When it is a question of ego, always drop it.
And if he is wrong, then laugh.
Then give him an
opportunity to grow.
