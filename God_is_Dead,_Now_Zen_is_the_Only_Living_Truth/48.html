NANGAKU SAID, ”YOUR QUESTION IS TOO ARROGANT.
Nobody asks such a question immediately. You entered into my temple and you started asking me

It was not arrogance, but that was part of the strategy of Seigen. Nangaku was a very different kind
of master.
NANGAKU SAID, ”YOUR QUESTION IS TOO ARROGANT. WHY DON’T YOU ASK MODESTLY?”
TO WHICH SEKITO REPLIED, ”THEN IT WOULD BE BETTER TO SINK INTO HELL ETERNALLY
AND NOT EVER HOPE FOR THE LIBERATION THAT THE OLD SAINTS KNOW.”
”If you call my question arrogant, then I would rather suffer eternally in hell than ask you any question
modestly.”
