Man has a center, but he lives off of it â€“ off the center. That creates an inner tension, a constant
turmoil, anguish. You are not where you should be; you are not at your right balance. You are off
balance, and this being off balance, off center, is the base of all mental tensions. If it becomes too
much, you go mad. A madman is one who has gone out of himself completely. The enlightened
man is just the reverse of the madman. He is centered in himself.
You are in between. You have not gone completely out of yourself, and you are not at your center
either. You just move in the gap. Sometimes you move very, very far away, so you have moments
when you are temporarily mad. In anger, in sex, in anything in which you have moved too far away
from yourself, you are temporarily mad. Then there is no difference between you and the madman.
The difference is only that he is permanently there and you are temporarily there. You will come
back.
When you are in anger it is madness, but it is not permanent. Qualitatively there is no difference;
