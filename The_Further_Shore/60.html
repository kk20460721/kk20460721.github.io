More and more people are extroverts, particularly people who are in your group. They are western
people; they are more extrovert. But even these extroverts, working, meditating, by and by become
introverts, and sometimes they too are at a stage where they can turn very easily into introverts.
So it depends – but don’t make it much of a consideration. If sometimes you feel like moving in that
space for one or two hours, then give that space to the group as a device. Let them also fall silent,
let them move slowly; let them keep pace with you. That will be enriching, that will be an enrichment.
