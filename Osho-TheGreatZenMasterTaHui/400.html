Anything that dies is not life; it is only the house in which life was living. The flame of life
goes on eternally. Because of your securities and consolations and beliefs you remain
unacquainted with the greatest treasure. You live in misery, you die in misery.
It is possible to live in immense bliss and to die dancingly, at ease with existence.
Okay, Maneesha?
Yes, Osho.
