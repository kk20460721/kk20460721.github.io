DYING, DYING, ALL GO ON DYING.
NONE DIE A PROPER DEATH.
KABIR MET WITH DEATH,
NEVER TO DIE AGAIN.
DYING, DYING, ALL GO ON DYING

DIE YOU MUST, SO DIE!
ALL WHIRLPOOLS FALL AWAY.
SUCH IS DEATH. SO WHY DIE
HUNDREDS OF TIMES A DAY?

LOVE CANNOT BE FELT.
