THEN WHY HAS THE WORLD ALWAYS BEEN IN CHAOS AND DISORDER?
THE WORLD has never been in chaos and disorder, only the mind. The world is absolutely orderly.
It is not a chaos, it is a cosmos. Only the mind is always in chaos, and will always be in chaos.
Something has to be understood: the very nature of the mind is to be in chaos because it is a
transitory stage. From nature to supernature mind is just a transition. No transitory stage can be in
order. How it can be in order? When you move from one stage to another, the inbetween is bound
to be in chaos.
There is no way to put the mind in order.
When you are transcending nature and moving into
supernature, changing from the outer to the inner, changing from the material to the spiritual, there
is bound to be a gap between the two when you are nowhere, when you don’t belong to this world
and you yet don’t belong to the other. This is the chaos – this has been left, and death has not been
achieved yet. In the middle, everything is a disorder. And if you remain in the middle, then you will
be always in chaos. Mind has to be transcended. It is not something to live with.
