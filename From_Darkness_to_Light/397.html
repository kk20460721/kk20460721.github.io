CHAPTER
29
History repeats itself, unfortunately
30 March 1985 pm in Lao Tzu Grove
Question 1
BELOVED OSHO,
AMERICA HAS BEEN LIKENED TO ROME PRIOR TO ITS FALL. IS IT APPROPRIATE TO SEE
THE FALL OF THESE TWO POWERS AS BEING CONNECTED TO THE PREDOMINANCE OF
CHRISTIANITY AT THE TIME?
History has moved, up to now, in circles. That is what is meant when it is said that history repeats
itself. It need not be so, it should not be so, but unfortunately it has been so up to now.
There is a certain law behind this phenomenon of history moving in circles.
I call it the law of
mediocrity, because anybody who has any intelligence could have seen that we have been making
the same kind of mistakes again and again and again. The mistakes are the same, the situations
are the same. It is unbelievable how mediocre the human mind is.
First let me explain to you the law of mediocrity. I have said to you that religion comes into existence
only when a society reaches the peaks of luxury.
Religion is the ultimate expression of all luxuries.
It is simple to understand and obvious.
There are physical needs which should be fulﬁlled ﬁrst,
because without the body the soul may melt and merge into the cosmos, but it cannot live on the
earth.
