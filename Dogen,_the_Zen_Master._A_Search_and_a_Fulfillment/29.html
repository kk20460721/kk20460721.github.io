”Twenty-ﬁve and a half inches, to be exact,” boasts Donald. ”And three inches in diameter.”
”Incredible,” says the optician. ”How much does it weigh?”
”Two and a half pounds,” is the proud reply.
”That is simply amazing!” exclaims the optician, unable to take his eyes off the magniﬁcent specimen.
”But why bring it to me? I’m an optician.”
”Well, you see,” says Donald, ”I have this problem. Every time I do one of these monsters, my eyes
water!”
