[Another group member said she felt she was receiving more love than she could bear... ]
It is, right. But your capacity to bear will increase every day. Love is always more than you can bear.
If it is there, it is always more than one can bear; if it is not there, it is always less than is needed.
If it is there it is always in abundance. God is really very spendthrift, not a miser. When one ﬂower
is needed, He will bloom a thousand and one ﬂowers. When one seed will do, He will throw millions
of seeds.
Love is too much, but the emptier you become, the more love you can absorb. And this is the beauty
– that one can become so empty that one can absorb the whole of God, the whole universe... the
whole.
