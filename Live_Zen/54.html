Maneesha, the dialogue between two enlightened persons is just playfulness. It does not matter
whether they sit silently together or speak. In that space, words or no words are equivalent.
There is nothing to say and nothing to hide but only a sharing, a sharing of the joy, a sharing of the
awakening, a sharing of the blissfulness, a sharing of their ecstasy. The awakened one becomes
just a child collecting seashells on the sea beach, or running after butterﬂies, or trying to catch the
rainbows.
