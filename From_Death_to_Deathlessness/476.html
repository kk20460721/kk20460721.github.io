Question 1
BELOVED OSHO,
WHAT ARE THE DEFECTS OF THE PRESENT POLITICAL SYSTEM IN INDIA, AND WHAT IS
THE REMEDY?
WHAT TYPE OF POLITICAL SYSTEM WOULD BE SUITABLE?
India is in a political mess. The reason lies in its past history.
Before the British government, India was never one country. It was divided into hundreds of small
kingdoms. There was no political system; the word of the king was the law. The credit goes to the
British government that it put it together and made India a country.
When India was struggling for freedom, my family has been continuously involved in the movement.
Everybody had been to jail, everybody had been beaten, everybody had suffered. But I was not in
favor of independence at that time. I said, ”Independence needs a certain clear conception of what
you are going to do after freedom. Freedom simply gives you the opportunity to do things, but you
have no idea what you are going to do after freedom. And this will bring a tremendous turmoil in the
whole continent.”
