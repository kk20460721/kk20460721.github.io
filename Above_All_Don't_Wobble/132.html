Watch a small child who is not yet corrupted by the society. When he is angry, he is really angry;
he explodes. A tiny child, but he becomes so powerful – as if he will destroy the whole world. He
becomes red, hot-red, as if he is on ﬁre. Just watch the child, how beautiful he is – so alive. And
next moment he is playing and laughing – the anger is no more there. You cannot even believe that
he was angry just a moment before. You cannot even suspect – this child, and angry? So loving, so
ﬂower-like – and just a moment before he was a ﬂame!
This is the way to live life. One day suddenly you have been repressing, it just never occurred. You
are, so totally, that there is never any hangover left from any moment. You are always fresh and
young, and the past is not like a load on you. You are not tired, frustrated by the past. This is what
is meant by dying every moment to the past and being born anew... a rebirth, a resurrection every
moment.
This is what I call a spiritual life. A spiritual life is not a life of discipline. It is a life of spontaneity.
Of course spontaneity has its own inner discipline, but to call it discipline is not good. It has its own
order, but to use the word order is not good because the word is misused too much. Good.
