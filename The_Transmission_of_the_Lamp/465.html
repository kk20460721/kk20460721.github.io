Question 7
BELOVED OSHO,
WHY DO PEOPLE BUG OUR TELEPHONES EVERYWHERE WE GO? ARE THEY LOOKING
FOR SPIRITUAL GUIDANCE ON THE CHEAP?
Certainly. Let them have it. We have nothing to hide. They can come and be here and enjoy, but

So whenever you are phoning just put in a few spiritual things for the buggers!
Question 8
BELOVED OSHO,
TO HAVE A HOT LOVER IS SOMETHING, BUT TO HAVE A HOT MASTER IS REALLY
SOMETHING ELSE! WHAT DO YOU SAY?
It is a very difﬁcult question. Geeta understood it. It is something else, because hot masters have
never been there. It is a totally new experience. Hot lovers have always been there, but hot lovers
get cold very soon. You cannot depend on them.
But a hot master is a hot master.
In fact I have to keep myself continuously air conditioned!
