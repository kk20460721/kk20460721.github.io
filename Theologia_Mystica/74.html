Mind can live only when you are unconscious.
To be conscious means the death of the mind
– and nobody wants to die.
Mind exists only when there is deep unconsciousness; the deeper
unconsciousness there is, the more nourishment for the mind.
A man goes to a doctor. The doctor’s wife leads him into the ofﬁce and explains that the doctor went
out and will be back shortly. Sitting down with the man, the wife starts ﬂirting with him. One thing
leads to another, and in no time at all she is giving him a blow-job.
Suddenly there is the noise of a car and in great distress the woman exclaims, ”Here’s my husband
– let me go!”
”No, no, please!” pleads the man. ”I am just coming – please continue a few seconds more!”
Just then they hear the key turning in the front door.
”Let me go! Let me go!” cries the woman.
”No, no, please! A few seconds more!”
But the woman violently tears herself away and manages to escape into the other room.
When the doctor enters, the man says, ”Good morning, doctor. I heard you coming so I decided to
undress in order to avoid wasting your time.”
”Good,” says the doctor, ”but what are you doing with those two ears in your hands?”
Man lives in unconsciousness.
He lives without knowing why, without knowing where, without
knowing whence. He simply goes on like a robot. And if you watch yourself you will be surprised
how you have lived up to now – almost in a dream.
