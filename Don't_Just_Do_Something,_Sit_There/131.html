So it is not that you are not ﬂowing with the river. You have started ﬂowing with the river – and the
mind still wants to go upstream; hence the dichotomy. The mind wants to go this way and you are

Now don’t listen to the mind. The mind is worthless because it is the past. Let the mind adjust with
you; don’t you adjust with the mind. The mind is your servant and he has to follow you. You are the
master and you need not follow the mind.
Yes, for a few days the mind will create trouble because it has been bossing you for such a long time
and it is difﬁcult to drop it so easily. It will resist, it will ﬁght; tooth and nail it will ﬁght. But if you go
