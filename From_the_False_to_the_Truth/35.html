You will be surprised... it has never happened before – all religions are against me. They have never

Belief is not needed, scriptures are not needed, prophets are not needed, God is not needed. A
tremendous courage to doubt everything is needed. It is your doubting capacity which will sharpen
your intelligence, destroy your mediocrity. But remember, with your mediocrity many other things will
be destroyed which depend on mediocrity. So beware, I am a dangerous man.
If you want to be intelligent you cannot be in politics. Politics needs idiots, people who can lie, people
who can go on lying, people who have double faces, people who can change their personality any
moment. They don’t have any individuality. If you want to be intelligent you cannot be in any politics.
If you want to be really religious you cannot be a member of any religious cult. You have to be free.
You have to be individual, you have to be on your own. It will be fearful in the beginning because
you have always lived in the crowd. The crowd gives you a certain coziness, warmth. You will be in
the cold, alone.
If you are ready to be alone and in the cold, and ready to go in darkness in search of light, religion
is yours.
