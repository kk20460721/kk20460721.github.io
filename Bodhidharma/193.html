I have heard that in a New York church, on a Sunday morning, the bishop came
a little early to see if all the arrangements were okay or not, because only on
Sunday do Christians become religious. It is a Sunday religion. As the bishop
entered he found a young man looking exactly like Jesus Christ. His heart sank.
He felt he was going to die. "My God, why has he come here? He has not learned
any lesson. When he came, what people did to him -- and again he is back! But it
is better to enquire who he is. Perhaps he is just a hippy who looks like Jesus." So
he went close to him and asked, "Who are you?"
Jesus looked at him and he said, "This is a strange question. You represent me on
the earth and you don't recognize me? I am Jesus Christ."
The bishop immediately phoned the Vatican to the pope saying, "What do you
think I should do? Jesus Christ is here in the church."
The pope said, "I am tortured so much with a thousand and one worries, and you
bring another. Can't you figure out what to do? The first thing is, inform the
police. And the second thing is, look busy."
