naturally you need not search for truth, you have it already.
It is absolutely necessary that God should be dead.
But I want you to know my understanding.
It was good of Friedrich Nietzsche to declare God dead. I declare that he has never been born.

Anybody who gives you a belief system is your enemy, because the belief system becomes the

just to take away your fear. But it is not taken away, it is only repressed in your unconscious. And
the deeper it is repressed the more difﬁcult it is to get rid of it.
