Who can be called a possessor of Tao? One who knows that he does not possess – that is the ﬁrst
condition to be fulﬁlled – one who knows that he is possessed by Tao, that he is no more, only Tao
is. But what will be the indication? How we will recognize that he possesses the Tao? He gives a
beautiful symbol to recognize the man of Tao. Ko Hsuan says:
ONLY WHEN HE CAN TRANSMUTE ALL LIVING THINGS CAN HE BE TRULY NAMED THE
POSSESSOR OF TAO.
The translation is again a little wrong. Ko Hsuan must have meant all living beings, not living things.
Things are not living, that’s why they are called things. Living beings... But why call living beings?
– because beings are naturally living. But things are complicated: all living beings are neither living
nor beings. Many living beings are dead – apparently living, only apparently, not really living.
