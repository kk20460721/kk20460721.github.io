In the Osho Meditation Center in Zurich, Switzerland, there was a clock-making
contest. Many beautiful clocks were made.
The third prize was won with a clock which had a cuckoo coming out every hour
saying, "Osho, Osho."
The second prize was won with a clock which had a cuckoo all dressed in orange with a
mala around his neck coming out every half hour saying, "Osho, Osho."
The first prize was given for a clock which had Osho coming out every fifteen minutes
saying, "Cuckoo, cuckoo."
Enough for today.
