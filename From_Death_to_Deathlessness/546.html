14 September 1985 am in Rajneeshmandir
Question 1
BELOVED OSHO,
EVERY TIME I AM ON MY OWN, I FEEL SEPARATE, LONELY, AND MISERABLE. I LOVE MYSELF
ONLY WHEN I AM WITH OTHERS. IF I AM ALONE, I FEEL ASHAMED AND DISLIKE MYSELF. IT
SEEMS AS IF I JUDGE MYSELF THROUGH THE EYES OF OTHERS.
