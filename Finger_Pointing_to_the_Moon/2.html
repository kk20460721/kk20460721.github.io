Don’t Just Listen, Do
13 October 1972 pm in Mt. Abu, Rajasthan, India
[NOTE: This is a translation of the Hindi series ADHYATMA UPANISHAD. This version is the ﬁnal
edit pending publication.]
INVOCATION
OM, MAY THE SUN GOD GIVE US HIS BENEDICTION.
MAY VARUNA, THE GOD OF WATER, GIVE US HIS BENEDICTION.
MAY ARYAMA, INDRA, BRAHASPATI AND
VISHNU GIVE US THEIR BENEDICTION.
MY SALUTATIONS TO BRAHMA, THE ABSOLUTE REALITY.
O VAYU, THE GOD OF AIR, SALUTATIONS ESPECIALLY TO YOU, BECAUSE YOU ARE THE
BRAHMA MANIFEST.
I SHALL CALL ONLY YOU THE MANIFEST BRAHMA.
I SHALL ALSO CALL YOU THE TRUTH, CALL YOU RIT – THE LAW.
MAY THEY PROTECT ME. MAY THEY PROTECT THE SPEAKER.
