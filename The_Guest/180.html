OSHO,

WHY SUCH A LONER ALL MY LIFE?
Prem Madhura,
LIFE is a mystery, but you can reduce it to a problem. And once you make a mystery a problem
you will be in difﬁculty, because there can be no solution to it. A mystery remains a mystery; it is
insoluble – that’s why it is called a mystery. Life is not a problem.
And that is one of the most basic mistakes we all go on committing: we immediately put a question
mark. And if you put a question mark on a mystery, you will be searching for the answer your whole
life and you will not ﬁnd it, and naturally it brings great frustration.
