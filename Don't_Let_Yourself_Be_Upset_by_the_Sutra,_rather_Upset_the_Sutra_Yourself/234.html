CHAPTER 51.
That is the story of Jesus’ resurrection. People cruciﬁed him – he was just like a ﬂower, crushed
by the rock – but he revived, resurrected. It is not an historical fact; it is a parable, a metaphor, a
beautiful metaphor, that the ﬂower will come back. You can go on crushing it again and again and
again – it will revive! It has eternity as its support. God is hidden behind the ﬂower, so only on the
surface can you crush it, you cannot destroy it forever. It has something of the immortal in it. It will
come back; in the right season, in the right moment, it will be there again.
