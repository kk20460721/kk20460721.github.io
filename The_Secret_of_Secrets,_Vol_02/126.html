CHAPTER 7. THE MOON GATHERS UP THE TEN THOUSAND WATERS
And when your ordinary heart has stopped and your ordinary pulse has stopped, then for the ﬁrst
time you feel a totally different quality starting. You again breathe, but the breathing is no more the
same. Your pulse again starts pulsating, but it is no more the same pulse. Now God lives in you.
Now you are not there, only God is.
That’s why we call Buddha ’Bhagwan’: a moment came when God started living in him. The man
disappeared. Then the man was just a hollow bamboo and the song of God started ﬂowing through
him. This is the ultimate goal.
