CHAPTER 50. ADHYATMA UPANISHAD
Move in, reach the third layer of consciousness.
Move away from body-matter, move away from
energy-mind.
Go deep to the ultimate core, to the last point of your existence, the center,
consciousness. You are a siddha. Why? Why a fulﬁlled one? – because then no desire arises,
because then no suffering is possible, because then you are constantly merged into bliss, because
then nothing is to be achieved.
You have achieved all that without which there is desire. You have achieved that one through which
everything is achieved. One becomes fulﬁlled, one becomes a siddha.
Now get ready for meditation.
