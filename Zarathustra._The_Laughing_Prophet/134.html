CHAPTER 10. BEFORE SUNRISE
To me, meditation is the way to the mystery of existence. He does not use the word meditation –
perhaps he was not even aware of the word – but what he says is exactly what meditation is.
Go beyond all your doubts, suspicions, questionings. Go into the dark and into the unknown and
into the mysterious – and with a deep yes and a sacred amen. That is the only way to know yourself,
and that is the only way to know the mysterious beauty of life and existence.
... THUS SPAKE ZARATHUSTRA.
