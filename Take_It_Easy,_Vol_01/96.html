SAT BODHI, SRI AUROBINDO IS A GREAT PHILOSOPHER but not a master at all. Very intelligent,
very scholarly, a great intellectual, but not an enlightened master.
He talks about God and talks
beautifully, but it is all talk, it is all abstraction. It is not a realization.
If it is a realization, then there can’t be any goal. For example, if the world has evolution as the goal,
then what will be the goal of evolution? Why evolution at all? Why in the ﬁrst place? You will have to
create another abstraction, and it will be a regress AD INFINITUM.
Whatsoever you ﬁx as the goal, the question will remain relevant about that. Why?
