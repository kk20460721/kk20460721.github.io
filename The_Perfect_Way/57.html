CHAPTER 7. THE THIRD EVENING
Truth is within you. It is within me. You don’t have to go anywhere else to look for it. You just have
to dig it out of yourselves as you dig wells for water.
You have to dig the well of the soul.
And
meditation is the spade for digging that well. With the spade of meditation we must dig out the earth
of the outside world that covers our nature – and then the one we are seeking is near. He is hidden
in the seeker himself.
