CHAPTER 2. THE DESIRELESS PATH OF YOGA
And it depends on your intensity. If your realization is so deep that it is total, that desire is misery,
you will not only have entered yoga, you have become a siddha. You have reached the goal.
It will depend on your intensity.
If the intensity is total, then you have reached the goal.
If your
intensity is not so total, you have entered the path.
