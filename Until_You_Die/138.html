CHAPTER
10
A Rose Is A Rose Is A Rose...
20 April 1975 am in Buddha Hall
A DISCIPLE CAME TO MARUF KARKHI AND SAID:
‘I HAVE BEEN TALKING TO PEOPLE ABOUT YOU. JEWS CLAIM THAT YOU ARE A JEW;
CHRISTIANS REVERE YOU AS ONE OF THEIR OWN SAINTS; MUSLIMS INSIST THAT YOU
ARE THE GREATEST OF ALL MUSLIMS.’
MARUF ANSWERED:
‘THIS IS WHAT HUMANITY SAYS IN BAGHDAD. WHEN I WAS IN JERUSALEM, JEWS SAID THAT
I WAS A CHRISTIAN, MUSLIMS THAT I WAS A JEW, AND CHRISTIANS THAT I WAS A MUSLIM.’
‘WHAT MUST WE THINK OF YOU THEN?’ SAID THE MAN.
MARUF SAID:
‘SOME DO NOT UNDERSTAND ME, AND REVERE ME. OTHERS DO NOT EITHER, SO THEY
REVILE ME. THAT IS WHAT I HAVE COME TO SAY. YOU SHOULD THINK OF ME AS ONE WHO
HAS SAID THIS.’
A RELIGIOUS man is always misunderstood. If he is not misunderstood, he will not be a religious
man.
Humanity lives in a non-religious attitude towards life – sectarian, but not religious. So a religious
man is a stranger.
And whatsoever you say about him will be wrong, because you are wrong.
