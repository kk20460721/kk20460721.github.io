CHAPTER 7. THE ESSENCE IS AT EASE...
The alchemy of stopping is what Zen is all about. If I can impart to you this stopping, if looking into
my eyes, if feeling my vibe, sometimes you can stop and drop all searching, God will come rushing
to you. You need not go to search for him, he is waiting for you to stop; he simply rushes into you
from all the sides. Man never reaches God. Whenever it happens, it happens the other way: man
stops searching and God comes to him. That has always been so, and its going to be always so.
Search not. Seek not, because all seeking is dreaming and all searching is desiring. And when
there is no desire, one has arrived.
