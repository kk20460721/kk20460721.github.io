CHAPTER 3. A VERY FRESH COMMUNION
OUR BELOVED MASTER,
DOES THE URGE TO UNDERSTAND EVER BECOME A VIOLATION OF THE MYSTERY OF
LIFE?
Maneesha, nothing can be a violation of the mystery of life, for the simple reason that there is no
solution. So you can do as much gymnastics, what people call mind-fucking, as you like, but the
mystery remains the same.

”Yes,” replies Fred. ”In Latin it was: ‘Veni, Vidi, Vici.’ It means: ‘I came, I saw, I conquered.’”
”Correct,” says Bill. ”And nowadays, all the young ‘Caesars’ have a different slogan – ‘Vidi, Vici, Veni’
