Ordinarily it is so – that is the ordinary case, for the
average it is so – but I don’t feel that about you. The mother is not standing between you and other

And there is no necessity that you should be angry and only then will things start happening, no.
Because sometimes it can happen that a mother may have been so beautiful that you don’t have
any anger. It happens! There are beautiful people in the world, and fortunately your mother may
have been such a person that you don’t have any anger. So don’t create a problem – I don’t think it
is so.
Rather than creating a problem, use the love towards your mother as a passage to other women –
because every woman is a mother. If you can love your mother, you can love the whole world of
women.
