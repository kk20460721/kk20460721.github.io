The man who had been selected for this college reached the place I was meant tO go to. He asked

I said, ”You enjoy it there, I will enjoy it here. Till they ﬁnd it out themselves you need not inform

And they were so happy, there was no question.
I asked her, ”Has paralysis happened any time?”
She said, ”No, no, paralysis, not at all. For these two years I have not even had a common cold. No
sickness has happened.”
