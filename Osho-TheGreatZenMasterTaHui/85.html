But such is the hypocrisy of the society that the same learned people who refused to accept
him as enlightened started saying that he had "renounced his body" -- not that he committed
suicide, not that he had committed a crime. Their actual word is jal samadhi: "He has dropped
into the water and become one with existence."
And still there exists a Ramateertha League, and there are followers ... and his books are
published, and people are reading those books in order to become enlightened.
Intellect can deceive you, can deceive others.
Beware of the intellect.
Beware of the mind.
Be very careful; don't be impressed easily. Certainly don't be impressed through intellect.
If suddenly a connection happens from being to being, that's another matter.
