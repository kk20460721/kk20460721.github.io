CHAPTER 25.
Truth is naked, with no masks, with no garments. And truth never promises. It delivers but it never
promises. To follow truth means to follow something without any expectation; to follow truth means
to go into the unknown, the uncharted. One never knows what is going to turn up. One never knows
where one is going to land.
To follow truth is to go into insecurity. But insecurity is freedom, insecurity is life; and insecurity is
another name for God.
