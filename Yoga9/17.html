This chapter is called vibhuti pada. Vibhtuti means "power." Patanjali included this
chapter so that his disciples and those who will ever be following him are made alert that
many powers happen on the way but you are not to get entangled with them. Once you
become entangled with power, once you are on a power trip, you arc in trouble. You will
be tied down to that spot -- and your flight will be stopped. And one has to go on flying
and flying till the very end, when the abyss opens and you are absorbed back into the
cosmic soul.
Let peace be thy goal.
