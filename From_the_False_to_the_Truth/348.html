creature! Come to my commune where nobody freaks out, everybody freaks in.

Question 6
BELOVED OSHO,
IT SEEMS TO ME THAT DEATH ENSLAVES US PHYSICALLY AND PSYCHOLOGICALLY. IT
ALLOWS US THE ILLUSION OF FREEDOM FOR SO MANY YEARS AND THEN STEPS IN TO
ASSERT ITS CLAIM ON US AND ON THOSE WE CARE FOR. I FEEL AT THE MERCY OF AN
IMMENSE AND WHIMSICAL PHENOMENON THAT MAKES A MOCKERY OF LIFE, LOVE AND
FREEDOM. AND IT FREAKS ME OUT.
So we have also one who freaks out? This is not allowed! If you enjoy freaking, why not freak in?
Freak as much as you can, but always keep the direction inwards.
And the reason you are freaking out is absolutely stupid. You think death gives you the illusion of
life, love, freedom, and then one day suddenly it comes in and everything is destroyed: life, love,
freedom. Death is not a reality. It cannot step in or step out.
Death never happens.
