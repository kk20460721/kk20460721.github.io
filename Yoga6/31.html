are to change the whole world; they have a mission to fulfill and do this and that.
I have no mission; I am not a missionary. I would like a world without
missionaries and without prophets, so that people can be left to live their own
lives. Prophets never allow. They are always after you -- with judgement. They
are always after you -- with ideas to be followed, comparison. They are always
there to throw you in hell or award you by heaven.
I have nothing -- no hell to throw you in and no heaven to give you -- just a
delight of being. And that is possible, simply possible. If you allow it to happen it
is possible.
To me, life is not a serious affair. In fact life is nothing but a gossip in the eternity
of existence, a gossip. I am gossiping here; you are listening, that's all. If you
enjoy you are here. If I enjoy I am here. If it becomes difficult to enjoy each other,
we separate -- no other bondage.
And I don't allow anybody -- he may even be a Padmasambhava -- to lay his trap
on me.
