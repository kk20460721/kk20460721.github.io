names, you will never know the reality. Existence is nameless. All names are given by us.

Mahatma Bhagwandin. I never agreed with Mahatma Gandhi, but with Mahatma Bhagwandin I had
a great friendship. He was very old and I was so young, but we both felt some synchronicity. So
whenever Mahatma Bhagwandin used to come to my city, he used to stay in our house. He was
a great scholar and immensely informed. I have never come across anybody who is so informed
about so much rubbish. You ask him anything and he will function almost like the ENCYCLOPEDIA
