Have you watched? Whenever you feel nervous, tense, you start smoking. It helps you
to cool down, to relax a little bit; otherwise life will become too much. When you are not
feeling nervous, when you are enjoying, when you are relaxed, you don't remember
smoking. Hours may pass and you may not smoke for the simple reason that there is no
cause. Otherwise you become afraid you may do something wrong. Better to keep
yourself engaged.
