CHAPTER 12. LOVE IS THE GREATEST ALCHEMY
His very being is laughter – that’s why there is no question of him laughing the way we laugh.
After enlightenment, every quality of life changes.
We have all those qualities but in a very raw condition. Enlightenment reﬁnes them, puriﬁes them,
and it takes some intelligence to recognize that they are the same qualities.
