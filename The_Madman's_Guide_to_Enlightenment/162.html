So for two, three months my feeling is that you should just be a child. Declare yourself to be a child.
Go into childish tantrums and [your wife] will have to look after you. Talk to her – if she feels it is
difﬁcult, send her to me; I will prepare her. For two, three months, do all that you always wanted
to do and which you have not done with your own mother. Within two, three months that will all go
down the drain, and you will come out more mature.
And there is not much of a problem – that is the only hitch. So talk tonight, mm? and surrender
completely.
