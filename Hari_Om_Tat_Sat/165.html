Veet Shastro, it raises a very historical question.
Zen was born in India in the absurd laughter
of Mahakashyapa, a close disciple of Gautam Buddha. He had many disciples even closer than
Mahakashyapa. Mahakashyapa is mentioned only once and that mention is of when he had laughed.
Mahakashyapa’s laughter was the beginning of Zen.
So ﬁrst you have to understand why
Mahakashyapa laughed. Why had he laughed?

in his hand. Everybody wondered – he had never before come with anything in his hand. And more
