CHAPTER 7. OSHO CONFRONTS THE FOLLOWERS OF MAHARISHI MAHESH YOGI
And one thing more Maharishi says: If you sit still the mind will become dull. If you do something, the
mind by the very doing becomes dull. If you don’t do anything, the mind is completely fresh, total;
how can it be dull? It cannot be dull. Mind can be made dull through any process. The very process
makes the mind dull because the process is a repetition. The repetition is yet dullness. Whenever
you repeat something you become dull. You go on repeating, go on repeating, repeating, repeating,
you become a dullness.
