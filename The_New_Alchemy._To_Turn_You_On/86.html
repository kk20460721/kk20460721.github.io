It always happens that when you have come nearer to the goal you feel more tired. You want to

back. And when you become aware again, you will ﬁnd that the temple has disappeared.
When the goal is near, don’t relax your effort. Rather, now bring your total energy to it; become totally
absorbed in it. This is the moment that the sutra is talking about when it says LOOK FORWARD
ALWAYS WITH AWE AND TREMBLING TO THIS MOMENT. Don’t relax.
