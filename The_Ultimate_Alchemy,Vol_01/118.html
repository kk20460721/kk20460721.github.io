CHAPTER 8. THE COMPLEMENTARINESS OF OPPOSITES
But the other path is of effort, tension. And many methods are possible, because to do something
there are many techniques. There are many techniques for how to create the ultimate tension so
that you explode. But never follow both. You cannot follow! You can just go on thinking about both.
And don’t confuse. Determine clearly, exactly, which is for you.
Can you trust? Are you ready without any ”how” to take the jump? If not, then forget relaxation, then
forget surrender, then even forget the very word – because you cannot understand it. Then effort –
and this Upanishad is talking about effort: upward effort, a continuous arrowing of the mind towards
the peak.
