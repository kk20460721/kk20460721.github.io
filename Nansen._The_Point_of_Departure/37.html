Chester Cheese is on a long sales trip when his car breaks down.
He walks up to a nearby
farmhouse and knocks on the door.
”Excuse me,” says Chester to the farmer, ”but my car has broken down.
Can I stay here for the
night?”
”Well, I guess so,” says the old man.
”But the only available bed belongs to my sixteen-year-old
daughter, Lucy. You will have to sleep with her.”
”Really?” smiles Chester. And he is shown to Lucy’s room.
Later that night, lying next to Lucy, Chester puts his hand on her soft thigh.
”Stop that!” cries Lucy. ”Or I will call my father.” But then she moves a little closer to Chester.
A little while later, Chester tries again. ”Stop that!” cries Lucy. ”Or I will call my father.” But then she
moves even closer to Chester.
On the third try, Lucy jumps on top of Chester, and they have a great time.
A little while later, Lucy whispers, ”Mister, can we do it again?”
Chester agrees, and they go for it again.
Five minutes later, Lucy is tugging again on Chester’s
deﬂated machinery. ”Mister, can we do it again?” she asks.
Stop that!” gasps Chester. ”Or I will call your father!”
That is what is happening in the outside world!
