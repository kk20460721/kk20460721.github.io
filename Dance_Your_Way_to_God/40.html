Primal therapy simply means taking people back to their childhood.
They will have to relive it in
imagination, and whatsoever has remained incomplete will have to be completed in imagination.

old wounds and to let them overpower you again, to again suffer those things that you had been
thinking had completely disappeared, but the wounds are there and they have to be healed. The
primal therapy group will take you on an inner journey.
