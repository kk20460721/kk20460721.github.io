Vigyan Bhairav Tantra, Vol 2
The Book of the Secrets: A New Commentary, The original series of 80 discourses were simply
called ”Vigyan Bhairav Tantra”. For publication as books they were divided up into 5 volumes,
called the ”The Book of the Secrets Volume 1 - 5” (16 discourses each). The books were later
published as ”Vigyan Bhairav Tantra Volumes 1 and 2”,(40 discourses each). The two volumes also
come with a deck of 112 cards to represent the various meditations.
Talks given from 25/03/73 pm to 08/11/73 pm
