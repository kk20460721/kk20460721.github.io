CHAPTER 28.
Shiva was meditating on the Himalayas. The god of love tempted him and he became very angry.
He opened his third eye and it was such a tremendous energy, it was ﬁre! He poured his ﬁre on the
god of love so that he was burned, his body was burned utterly, forever. Since then he has been
without a body, he moves amongst people with no body.
So love uses your body, your lover’s body; hence the cause of misunderstanding. But it is love that
demands sacriﬁce. Love is not possible without sacriﬁce. It exists only when there is utter sacriﬁce
with no condition, with no bargain
