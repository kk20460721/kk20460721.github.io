inﬂuenced by the crowd. Try to remain aloof, try to remain yourself. Only then is there a possibility

ecstasy, a new dance, a new song will overwhelm you.
That new dawn is very close, you just have to remember to be yourself, authentically, sincerely. Up
to now mankind has lived as a crowd. From now onwards, if mankind wants to live at all it has to live
