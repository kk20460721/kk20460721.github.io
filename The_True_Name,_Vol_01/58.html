CHAPTER 3. SOLVING THE RIDDLE
Kabir says: ”Only he who is prepared to burn his house, need walk with me.” Which house is Kabir
talking of? The ediﬁce you have created round yourself – the house of lies, deceit, anger, enmity,
malice, jealousy and hatred – this is what your house is.
Grab hold of this sutra, only this:
HE IS THE BENEFACTOR OF ALL. LET ME NEVER FORGET HIM.
That within, the master of all, is one. For your life to change, you need nothing more. You do not
need to do Patanjali’s yoga asanas, nor have you to worry about the ten commandments of the
Jews, nor concern yourself with the Gita or the Koran.
A small secret, such a tiny secret, to change your entire life! Through this secret Nanak attained and
you can also.
But remember Kabir: ”Only he who is prepared to burn his house, he alone need walk with me.”
