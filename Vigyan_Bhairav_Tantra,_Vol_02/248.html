Watching from the hills
30 June 1973 pm in Bombay, India
SUPPOSE YOU CONTEMPLATE SOMETHING
BEYOND PERCEPTION,
BEYOND GRASPING,
BEYOND NOT BEING.
– YOU.
I AM EXISTING.
THIS IS MINE.
THIS IS THIS.
OH BELOVED,
EVEN IN SUCH KNOW ILLIMITABLY.
Man is Janus-Faced – animal and divine both. Animal belongs to his past, divine belongs to his
future, and this creates the difﬁculty. The past has passed, it is no more; just a shadow of it lingers
on.
And the future is still the future, it has not yet come; it just a dream, just a possibility.
