CHAPTER 7. ANY MOMENT!
The prisoner thanked the sultan for being given a chance to live, and then proceeded to the ﬁrst tent.
In ten minutes he emerged staggeringly drunk, holding upside-down an empty wine jug in one hand.
On unsteady feet he plunged into the second tent. Seconds later, everyone’s blood curdled because
of the terrible screams and roars which came forth from that tent. About eight minutes later, the
prisoner emerged from the second tent, a horrible bloody mess, covered with long scratches, deep
bites and fearful gouges from the tiger’s claws. To look at him was sickening.
Reeling up to one of the royal attendants, the prisoner demanded: ”Now where is the girl with the
toothache?”
