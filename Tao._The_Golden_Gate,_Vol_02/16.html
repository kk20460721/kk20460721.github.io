CHAPTER 1. MAN IS A BECOMING
When the ten monks had again formed into a line, a lovely, naked, curvaceous blonde stepped out
and sauntered along the line of men. Nothing happened until she got to the end of the line when
again... ting-a-ling, ting-a-ling went the bell of the last monk, this time more excitedly than before.
It was only through the compassion of the Father Superior that number ten was allowed one ﬁnal
chance, but if he failed again he would be sent away with no further discussion.
When the ten monks lined up for the third time, a most beautiful naked redhead appeared and
temptingly walked along the line. When she got to number ten it was more than he could bear, and
his bell rang so violently that it broke its cord and fell to the ground.
Totally dejected, number ten had to accept his fate of being sent out of the Order.
As he stepped forward and bent over to pick up his bell, there was suddenly heard the sound of nine
ringing bells – ting-a-ling, ting-a-ling, ting-a-ling...
