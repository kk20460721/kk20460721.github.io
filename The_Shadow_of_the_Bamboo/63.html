this is just the way you are...
I am knowingly not saying anything about the consequence, because then the acceptance will be
false: you will be focused on the consequence, the result. Simply accept out of helplessness, not
for a certain result.
Something is bound to happen, because acceptance is such a great force; something is absolutely
certain to happen. But what it will be, I cannot say, and I should not say and you should not ask.

become such a part of your being. It has been a kind of marriage and divorce is painful
....

be there; nothing can be done. When it does not come, enjoy; when it comes, accept.
Let us see what happens out of acceptance. Something is bound to happen, something that may
be a surprise to you!
