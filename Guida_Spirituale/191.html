”And what about money?” inquired Suzy’s mother.
”Well,” said Suzy, ”I get one dollar a week and Joey gets one dollar a week, and I have always heard
you say that two can live cheaper than one.”
”And what about children? Have you thought about having children?” asked Joey’s mother.
