Having come from a small village, Giovanni had never been a train in his life. So one day he decided
to go and see one. Standing on the rail he heard the train whistle, ”Tooo-tooo!” Before he knew what
hit him, the train was upon him and knocked him off the rail. The last thing he could remember was
ﬂying through the air.
When he gained consciousness, Giovanni found himself laid up in the hospital for several months.
During this time his wife used their accident insurance money to buy a well-equipped, modern ﬂat in
the city.
Once fully recovered, Giovanni went to his new home. His wife made a cake to celebrate his recovery
and put the new kettle on the stove to make some coffee to go with the cake. As the water boiled,
the kettle whispered, ”Tooo-tooo!” Startled, Giovanni jumped up, rushed to the kitchen, grabbed the
new kettle and smashed it on the ﬂoor, kicking it several times.
